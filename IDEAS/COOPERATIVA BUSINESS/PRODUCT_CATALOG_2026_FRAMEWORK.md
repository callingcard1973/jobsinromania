## 1. ABOUT GOSPODARII DE ALTADATA

**Gospodarii de Altadata** is an official cooperative aggregator of Romanian mountain producers registered in the National Registry of Mountain Products (RNPM).

### Our Mission
Connect 680+ certified mountain producers with European buyers through transparent, compliant supply chains.

### What We Do
- **Aggregation**: Consolidate supply from multiple small farms into reliable volumes
- **Quality Assurance**: Every product certified for HACCP, FSSC, Produs Montan standards
- **Export Logistics**: Handle customs, documentation, shipping, cold chain
- **Traceability**: Full batch tracking with QR codes (EU 178/2002 compliant)

### Key Statistics
- **680 certified producers** across Romanian mountain regions
- **1,186 partner cooperatives** for supply scaling
- **EU protected designation** (Produs Montan = premium category)
- **Single invoice advantage** (buyer convenience)
- **Shipped to 9 EU countries** in 2025-2026

## 3. CERTIFICATIONS & COMPLIANCE

### Produs Montan (EU Protected Designation)
- **What**: Mountain product designation (protected under EU law)
- **Value**: 30-50% premium pricing justified
- **Your Benefit**: Authentic branding + consumer trust

### HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points)
- International food safety standard
- Required for all EU food exports
- ✅ All our producers certified

### FSSC 22000 (Food Safety System Certification)
- Comprehensive food safety auditing
- ✅ Majority of producers hold this

### EU 178/2002 (Traceability Regulation)
- Every batch has unique ID + movement tracking
- QR code on packaging = transparency
- ✅ We provide this as standard

### Organic Certification (Select products)
- Some products certified organic
- +15-25% price premium
- Growing EU demand
