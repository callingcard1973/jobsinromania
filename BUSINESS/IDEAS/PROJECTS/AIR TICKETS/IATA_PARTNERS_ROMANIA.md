Sub-agent operates under host agency's IATA accreditation. Host handles BSP settlement, you handle sales. Commission split typically 1-3% of ticket value to sub-agent, on top of the airline's agent commission (7-9% on ethnic routes).

## Legal Requirements (Romania)

- **Tourism License (Licenta de turism)**: Required if you collect payment and deliver tickets. Issued by Ministry of Tourism under OG 107/1999.
- **CAEN code**: 7911 (travel agency) or 7912 (tour operator) — register at ONRC
- **Financial guarantee**: Insurance or bank guarantee covering client funds
- **ANAT membership**: Optional but adds credibility. Few hundred EUR/year.

## Shortcut: Affiliate-Only (No License Needed)
