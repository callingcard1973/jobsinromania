# Research: ASOCIATII (IDEA-025)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Registry of 140,664 active Romanian NGOs across 42 counties. Lead generation, EU funding partnerships, campaign targeting. Data from Ministry of Justice (weekly updates).

## Market Size
- Romania has ~4,200-4,300 new NGO registrations per year
- 140,664 active NGOs (118K associations, 19.7K foundations, 1.6K federations)
- Global nonprofit data market led by Candid/GuideStar (1.8M US nonprofits, $1,199/yr premium)
- No European equivalent to GuideStar exists at scale
- GlobalGiving Atlas lists 142,128 Romanian nonprofits (free, limited data)
- NGO sector spending in Romania: estimated EUR 1-2B annually

## Competitors
| Competitor | What | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|------|-------|----------|
| Ministry of Justice registry | Official XLSX dumps | Free | Raw data, no enrichment, no analytics |
| GlobalGiving Atlas | 142K RO nonprofits, API/CSV | Free | Basic data, no emails/phones, US-focused |
| ngobase.org | NGO directory by country | Free | Minimal data, no enrichment |
| NGO Explorer | Country-level NGO stats | Free | Aggregate only, no individual records |
| Lusha | B2B contacts including NGOs | $36/mo | Thin Romania coverage |
| Candid/GuideStar | US nonprofit data leader | $1,199/yr | US only, no Romania data |
| FDSC (Civil Society Foundation) | Romanian NGO sector reports | Free reports | Research only, no lead lists |

## Our Advantage
- 140,664 enriched records (emails, phones, CUI, CAEN matched)
- Priority-scored shortlist of 5,001 leads ready for outreach
- 43 county-level CSV files for targeted campaigns
- Company matching pipeline (exact + fuzzy name matching against company DB)
- Weekly refresh from Ministry of Justice
- No competitor offers enriched Romanian NGO data at this scale

## Validated?
Partially. Demand exists: EU funding consultants, CSR departments, and NGO service providers need targeted NGO lists. Candid/GuideStar proves the model at $1,199/yr in the US. No direct customer validation for Romania-specific NGO data.

## Price
- County pack (1 county): EUR 29
- Regional pack (8 counties): EUR 79
- National (all 42 counties): EUR 149
- Enriched + scored leads (5,000): EUR 199
- API access: EUR 49/month

## Risk
- LOW-MEDIUM: Data is public record (Ministry of Justice)
- GDPR applies to personal data in NGO records (contact persons)
- Small market: who actually pays for Romanian NGO data?
- Revenue ceiling likely EUR 500-2,000/month
- Free alternatives (GlobalGiving, Ministry) reduce willingness to pay

## Recommendation
**CONDITIONAL GO** - The data asset is strong but the buyer market is narrow. Best use: (1) Campaign targeting for own EU funding consulting (cifn.eu), (2) Cross-sell with EBRD/EU procurement campaigns, (3) Partnership outreach for cooperative (IDEA-011). Selling standalone NGO data is low-revenue. Use it as a lead gen tool for higher-value services.
