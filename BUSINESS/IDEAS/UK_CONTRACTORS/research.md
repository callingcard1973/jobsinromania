# Research: UK CONTRACTORS (IDEA-034)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Target 34 major UK contractors with sponsor licenses + 2,597 construction companies via Find a Tender API. Offer Romanian construction workers for UK projects requiring Skilled Worker visas.

## Market Size & Demand
- UK construction sector faces chronic skilled labour shortage — actively recruiting overseas
- Skilled Worker Visa is primary route: up to 5 years, pathway to permanent residency
- UK government publishes Register of Licensed Sponsors (updated April 15, 2026) — publicly available
- Sponsor licence cost: GBP 611 (small) / GBP 1,682 (medium/large) — employers already invested
- Construction listed as "higher-risk sector" for sponsorship — employers need reliable agency partners
- Multiple UK recruitment agencies already specializing in Eastern European workers (Vira International, Workforce Group)

## Market Validated?
YES — Multiple agencies already operating profitably in this exact niche. UK government actively facilitating overseas construction recruitment through Skilled Worker Visa route. Chronic labour shortage confirmed across industry sources.

## Price Point
- Placement fee: GBP 2,000-8,000 per worker (industry standard)
- Typical margin: 15-25% of annual salary
- UK construction worker salary: GBP 25,000-45,000/year
- Revenue per placement: GBP 3,750-11,250
- With 34 major contractors, even 1 placement each = GBP 127K-382K

## Risk
- MEDIUM: UK immigration rules change frequently (visa requirements, salary thresholds)
- Need UK-based legal entity or partnership for sponsor compliance
- Competition from established agencies with UK presence
- Worker quality control and retention challenges
- Brexit regulatory complexity still evolving

## Recommendation
**LAUNCH** — Highest revenue potential of all 6 ideas. Market is proven, demand is structural (UK labour shortage), and we have unique assets (contractor database + worker pipeline). Start with email campaign to 34 major contractors offering Romanian construction workers. No development needed — just outreach + matching. Partner with UK immigration solicitor for compliance. Target: 5 placements in first 3 months = GBP 18K-56K revenue.
