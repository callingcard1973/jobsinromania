# Research: CUMPARFERME (IDEA-013)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Brokerage for bankrupt farm assets in Romania. Leverage database of 8,663 farms + 192K companies to match distressed agricultural assets with buyers. Commission-based model on asset sales.

## Market Size & Demand
- 120+ large Romanian agricultural companies went bankrupt in 2024 alone; 123 insolvency requests Jan-Sep 2024
- Drought destroyed over half of Romania's agricultural land in 2024, accelerating farm failures into 2025-2026
- Romania agricultural land market is active: listings from 500 HA to 6,900 HA on investmentsinromania.eu
- ANAF launched eLicitatiiANAF platform (March 2026) — 14 million visits on day one, proving massive demand for distressed asset auctions
- No pre-pack sale mechanism in Romanian insolvency law — assets only sold during active proceedings

## Competitors Found

| Competitor | What | Pricing | Threat |
|------------|------|---------|--------|
| investmentsinromania.eu | Farm/land listings for foreign investors | Commission-based | Medium — general listings, not insolvency-focused |
| licitatii-insolventa.ro (UNPIR) | Official insolvency auction portal | Free access | High — official source but poor UX, no brokerage |
| eLicitatiiANAF | ANAF digital auction platform (new 2026) | Free | High — government platform, huge traffic |
| licitatia.ro | Public auction monitoring + alerts | Subscription | Medium — monitors SEAP/ANAF but not farm-specific |
| executari-insolvente.ro | Aggregator for enforcement/insolvency sales | Subscription | Medium |
| viviun.com | International farm/land marketplace | Listing fees | Low — not Romania-focused |

## Our Advantage
- 8,663 farms + 192K companies already in database with contact info and financials
- Can proactively match buyers to distressed assets before public auction (higher margins)
- Email campaign infrastructure already built (2,500+/day capacity)
- Domain cumparlegume.com + agroevolution.com already live with agricultural audience
- Can combine with MADR land data (9,658 listings) for comprehensive coverage

## Market Validated?
YES — ANAF's 14M visits on day one proves massive demand for distressed asset sales. 120+ farm bankruptcies in 2024 with rising trend. Active foreign investor interest in Romanian farmland.

## Price Point
- Brokerage commission: 2-5% of transaction value (standard for asset sales)
- Average farm asset sale: EUR 50K-500K
- Per-deal revenue: EUR 1,000-25,000
- Alert/monitoring subscription: EUR 50-200/month (recurring)

## Risk
- MEDIUM-HIGH: Insolvency sales are legally regulated — must work with licensed practitioners (UNPIR members)
- Competition from government platform eLicitatiiANAF (free, massive adoption)
- Long sales cycles (insolvency proceedings take months/years)
- Requires legal expertise or partnership with insolvency practitioners

## Recommendation
**BUILD** — Market demand is proven (ANAF 14M visits). The angle is NOT competing with auction platforms but offering brokerage services: finding buyers, matching assets, providing data enrichment. Partner with 2-3 insolvency practitioners, start with farm equipment and land (highest value). Use existing email infrastructure to reach potential buyers. Revenue potential EUR 5K-20K per deal.
