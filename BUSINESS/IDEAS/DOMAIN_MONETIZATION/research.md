# Research: DOMAIN MONETIZATION (IDEA-064)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
15 job domains (.eu) with SEO traffic. Add affiliate links (Indeed, Booking.com, Travelpayouts). Zero effort after setup.

## Market Size & Demand
- Affiliate marketing industry worth $17B+ globally (2026)
- Job/recruitment affiliate is a sub-niche; Indeed is the main program
- Travel affiliate (Booking, Travelpayouts) is larger and better-paying
- EU job domains have natural SEO value for "jobs in [country]" queries

## Competitors Found

| Affiliate Program | Commission | Cookie | Requirements | Notes |
|------------------|-----------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Indeed Publisher | $0.10/click | 30 days | 10K unique views/day | Very low CPC, high volume needed |
| Booking.com | 25-40% of their 15% commission | Session only | Website approval | ~3-6% of booking value |
| Travelpayouts | Varies by partner | 30 days | Website approval | Aggregates 80+ travel brands |
| Awin (travel) | Varies | 30 days | EUR 5 signup + approval | Network with 1000s of advertisers |
| CJ Affiliate | Varies | 30 days | Website approval | Large network |
| Google AdSense | $1-5 RPM (job niche) | N/A | Website approval | Display ads, passive |

## Our Advantage
- 15 .eu job domains already owned and hosted
- SEO content already deployed (articles in 11 languages)
- Zero additional cost -- domains already paid
- Can combine job affiliate + travel affiliate (workers relocating = need hotels/flights)
- Existing article generation pipeline can create affiliate-optimized content

## Market Validated?
PARTIALLY. Indeed affiliate pays very poorly ($0.10/click, need 10K views/day minimum). Real case: $164 earned from 50K pageviews. Booking.com is better but needs session-only conversions. The model works at scale but our traffic is likely too low for meaningful income from Indeed alone.

## Price Point
Not a subscription product. Revenue depends on traffic:
- 1K visitors/day across all domains: ~EUR 50-150/mo (AdSense)
- 5K visitors/day: ~EUR 200-500/mo (mixed affiliate)
- 10K+ visitors/day: EUR 500-1500/mo (Indeed + Booking + AdSense)
Reality check: most niche .eu domains get 10-100 visitors/day without serious SEO investment.

## Risk
- LOW financial risk (zero cost to implement), but LOW reward too
- Indeed requires 10K unique/day -- most domains won't qualify
- Booking.com session-only cookie kills most conversions
- SEO takes 6-12 months to build meaningful traffic
- Revenue likely EUR 50-300/mo total across all domains

## Recommendation
**BUILD** -- Do it because it costs nothing, but set expectations low. Start with AdSense + Booking.com on top 5 traffic domains. Don't count on this as a revenue stream. It's passive income that compounds over time as SEO grows. Register for Travelpayouts + Awin + CJ now (already planned per MEMORY.md).
