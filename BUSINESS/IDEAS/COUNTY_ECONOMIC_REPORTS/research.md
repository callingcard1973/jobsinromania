# Research: COUNTY ECONOMIC REPORTS (IDEA-127)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Automated quarterly reports per Romanian county: new companies, insolvencies, top sectors, employment, investment. EUR 200/report. Target: banks, consultancies, RE developers, local government.

## Market Size
- 42 Romanian counties (41 + Bucharest)
- ~500 potential B2B buyers (banks, consultancies, chambers of commerce, RE developers)
- Comparable: OECD reports ($150-300), FocusEconomics ($1,500/yr)
- County-level economic reporting in Romania is underserved

## Competitors
| Competitor | Type | Price | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|-------|
| FocusEconomics | Country-level | $1,500/yr | 36 analysts, no county detail |
| OECD Economic Surveys | Country-level | EUR 50-100/report | No county granularity |
| INS (Nat. Statistics) | Government | Free | Raw data, delayed 3-6 months |
| World Bank Romania | Reports | Free | Macro only |
| Erste Group Research | Banking | Free/clients | National forecasts only |
| Coface Romania | Risk reports | Enterprise | Sector focus, not county |
| Local chambers of commerce | Ad-hoc | Varies | Inconsistent, not automated |

## Our Advantage
- 208M companies in DB with county-level data
- Real-time insolvency from BPI, ANOFM employment per county
- Auto-generate 168 reports/year (42 x 4 quarters), near-zero production cost
- No competitor at county level

## Validated?
PARTIALLY. No direct competitor = untapped opportunity or insufficient demand. Banks compile this manually. Must test: sell to 5 county chambers.

## Price
EUR 200/report. EUR 600/yr (quarterly, 1 county). EUR 2,000/yr (all 42). Enterprise EUR 5,000/yr + API.

## Risk
- MEDIUM-HIGH: Unproven demand. Buyers may expect free. Automated reports may seem "thin."

## Recommendation
CAUTIOUS GO. Build 3 samples (Bucharest, Cluj, Harghita). Pitch to 5 banks + 5 consultancies. If 2+ buy, scale. Near-zero production cost.
