Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Client provides 5 competitor CUIs. We track SEAP contracts, ANAF changes, insolvency risk, hiring activity. EUR 99/month. Data already in our DB.

## Our Advantage
- Integrated view: SEAP + ANAF + insolvency + hiring in ONE dashboard
- Termene.ro does ANAF but not SEAP. Licitatia.ro does SEAP but not ANAF. We do BOTH.
- Already have the data (208M companies, SEAP scrapers, insolvency alerts on cifn.eu)
- EUR 99/mo is competitive vs. buying Termene + Licitatia separately (~EUR 80-150/mo)
- LLM analysis layer (summarize changes, risk scoring) = unique differentiator

## Validated?
Yes. Termene.ro and Licitatia.ro prove Romanians pay for business monitoring. Our combined offering fills a gap.

## Price
- Starter: EUR 49/mo (3 CUIs, basic alerts)
- Pro: EUR 99/mo (5 CUIs, full SEAP+ANAF+insolvency+hiring)

## Recommendation
GO. Build MVP dashboard combining existing SEAP + ANAF + insolvency data. Launch as feature of cifn.eu (already has insolvency). Target construction companies (already in our campaigns). EUR 99/mo x 50 clients = EUR 60K/year achievable in 12 months.
