# Research: CATALOAGE DOMENII (IDEA-046)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
9 domains (careworkers.eu, factoryjobs.eu, buildjobs.eu, etc.) x 20 countries with employer catalogs generated from existing DB. Monetize via job posting fees, featured listings, or subscription access.

## Market Size & Demand
- Global job board market: $28B (2025)
- Niche job boards charge $99-$600/post; featured listings 2-3x base price
- Just Join IT (EU tech board): grew from EUR 754K (2019) to EUR 14.5M (2022)
- RemoteOK charges $600/post; GirlBoss $299-$550/post
- 60% of job boards rely solely on posting fees; best ones combine 3-4 revenue streams
- Niche boards profitable within 6-12 months with lower marketing costs

## Competitors Found
| Competitor | Focus | Revenue Model | Scale |
|-----------|-------|---------------|-------|
| Just Join IT | EU tech jobs | Job postings + featured | EUR 14.5M/yr |
| RemoteOK | Remote jobs | $600/post | 50K+ visitors |
| OnlineJobs.ph | Philippines workers | Employer subscriptions | 3M+ profiles |
| EURES | EU cross-border jobs | Free (EU-funded) | All EU countries |
| Indeed/LinkedIn | General | Freemium + sponsored | Dominant |
| GirlBoss | Niche (women) | $299-$550/post | Medium |

## Our Advantage
- Already have 9 live domains with SEO authority across specific sectors
- Database of 2,665+ employers already enriched with contacts
- 20-country multilingual content already generated (11 languages)
- Zero customer acquisition cost for catalog content (data exists)
- Sector-specific domains (careworkers, factoryjobs, buildjobs) have high intent SEO value

## Market Validated?
YES. Job boards are a proven $28B market. Niche boards consistently outperform general ones on conversion. Multiple examples of bootstrapped niche boards reaching $1M+ revenue.

## Price Point
- Free basic listing (drives traffic)
- EUR 99-299/featured employer listing per country
- EUR 49-149/month employer subscription (dashboard + analytics)
- EUR 500-2000/year premium placement across all 20 countries

## Risk
- LOW-MEDIUM. Content exists, domains exist. Main risk is traffic generation and employer willingness to pay for listings on new platforms
- EURES is free (EU-funded) so cannot compete on price for basic listings
- Need critical mass of job seekers before employers will pay

## Recommendation
GO. Low investment (auto-generate from DB), high upside. Start with 3 strongest domains (factoryjobs, buildjobs, careworkers), 5 countries each. Monetize via featured listings + Google Ads initially. Add employer subscriptions once traffic >5K/month per domain.
