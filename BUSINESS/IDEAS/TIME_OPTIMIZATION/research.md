Invata cand cumpara oamenii. Optimizeaza timing emailuri + promotii. Date din pipeline-ul existent de 6,300 emails/zi.

## Our Advantage
- Already sending 6,300 emails/day with months of data
- Can build ML model on own data (more relevant than generic)
- Brevo already offers basic STO -- can layer our own on top
- Country/timezone data from employer database
- Zero cost vs $80+/mo for Seventh Sense

## Validated?
YES -- Every major ESP offers STO as premium. Studies show 20-30% open rate improvement.

## Price
Internal tool. Expected lift: 15-25% better open rates = more responses = more placements.

## Risk
- LOW: Enhancement to existing system
- B2B send time is well-studied (Tue-Thu 9-11 AM)
- Diminishing returns on optimization

## Recommendation
BUILD LATER -- Good optimization but not urgent. B2B timing well-known. Implement after campaigns stable. Simple: analyze reply timestamps, cluster by country/sector.
