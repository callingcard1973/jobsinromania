# Research: DATA CLEANING SERVICE (IDEA-082)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
CSV murdar -> curat: dedup + validare email + normalizare telefon + ANAF enrichment. EUR 0.02-0.10/record. Pipeline exista deja.

- B2B data quality market: multi-billion globally
- Per-record pricing: $0.01 (PDL raw) to $2+ (ZoomInfo low volume)
- Enterprise platforms: $50K-250K+/yr (Informatica, Experian, Talend)
- Growing 15-20%/yr driven by AI and compliance needs

## Our Advantage
- Pipeline already built and running (dedup, email validation, phone normalization)
- ANAF enrichment unique to Romania (CUI lookup, NACE codes, financial data)
- 208M records = benchmark database for dedup matching
- Zero marginal cost (own infrastructure)
- Romanian market has no local data cleaning SaaS

## Market Validated?
YES. ZoomInfo charges $15K-40K/yr. Melissa $1K+/yr. Our EUR 0.02-0.10/record is accessible to Romanian SMEs. No local competitor in RO.

## Price Point
- Per record: EUR 0.02 (basic dedup) to EUR 0.10 (full enrichment)
- Minimum order: EUR 50 (500 records)
- Monthly subscription: EUR 200/mo (10K records/mo)
- Enterprise: EUR 500/mo (unlimited + API access)
- 100K records x EUR 0.05 = EUR 5,000 per job

## Risk
- Low per-unit revenue requires volume
- Client education needed (many RO firms don't know data quality)
- Support overhead for messy edge cases
- Competitors could add RO/ANAF enrichment

## Recommendation
GO. Pipeline exists, unique ANAF angle, no local competitors. Build simple upload portal. Market to agencies and e-commerce firms.
