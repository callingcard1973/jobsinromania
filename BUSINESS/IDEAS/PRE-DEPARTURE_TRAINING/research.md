# Research: PRE-DEPARTURE TRAINING (IDEA-113)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Pre-departure training for workers going to Norway: language, safety, culture. Partner with AJOFM or private trainers. EUR 100-300/worker. 100 workers/fair = EUR 10K-30K.

## Market Size
- IOM runs pre-departure orientation globally: 800,000+ migrants trained in 19 years, 100K+ in 2023 alone
- Corporate expat training: $2,000-5,000/person (executive level)
- Blue-collar worker training: EUR 50-300/person (language + safety basics)
- Norway requires HMS (health/safety) card for construction: mandatory training exists

## Competitors
| Competitor | Price | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| IOM CIOP | Free (donor-funded) | 140 nationalities, government-backed |
| NRI Relocation | $2,950+ | Corporate expat focus, 10-day programs |
| ARC Relocation | Custom | Executive training, cultural coaching |
| Aperian (GlobeSmart) | Custom | Online cultural training platform |
| Preply (corporate) | $30-60/hr | Language tutoring, 1:1 online |
| AJOFM Romania | Free | Basic job readiness, no country-specific |
| Norwegian language schools RO | EUR 200-500/course | General, exist in Bucharest |

## Our Advantage
- Can bundle with job placement (IDEA-096/099): "train + place" package
- Specific to Norway construction (not generic expat training)
- Can deliver online (low cost) via recorded modules + live Q&A
- AJOFM partnership possible (they train but lack Norway-specific content)
- Content created once, sold repeatedly (scalable)

## Validated?
Yes. IOM proves demand. Norway HMS card requirement makes safety training mandatory. Gap: no Romania-specific "going to Norway" package exists commercially.

## Price
- Basic (online, self-paced): EUR 50-100
- Standard (online + 2 live sessions): EUR 150-200
- Premium (in-person 3-day + certificate): EUR 250-300
- Agency bulk discount: EUR 80/worker for 20+ workers

## Risk
Medium. Requires content creation investment. No official accreditation unless partnering with recognized body. Workers may not pay themselves; agencies or AJOFM should subsidize. Seasonal demand.

## Recommendation
CONDITIONAL GO. Create online modules first (low cost). Partner with AJOFM Harghita (existing relationship) for in-person delivery. Sell to agencies as part of placement package. Revenue realistic at EUR 20K-50K/year if bundled with placements.
