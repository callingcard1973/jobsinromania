# Research: GUMROAD ALL IDEAS BUNDLE (IDEA-105)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Sell master inventory as "European Business Intelligence Kit" on Gumroad. 95 documented ideas + data samples + scripts. EUR 499 one-shot.

## Market Size
- Gumroad: 140K+ products, best sellers are templates, courses, data packages
- Business intelligence data market: ~$30B globally (2025)
- Digital product bundles on Gumroad: typically $29-499 range
- "Business Bundle Pro" on Gumroad offers 150+ tools as lifetime access

## Competitors
| Competitor | Price | What |
|-----------|-------|------|
| GumroadData/Gumtrends | $49-199 | Gumroad-specific analytics |
| Business Bundle Pro | ~$99 | 150+ biz tools bundle |
| Various "lead list" sellers | $49-299 | Company databases, email lists |
| Veridion/ZoomInfo | $10K+/yr | Enterprise company data |
| European company registries | Free-$500 | Raw OpenData |

## Our Advantage
- Unique: no one sells a curated EU recruitment intelligence package
- 208M companies, 440K emails, 18K agencies already in DB
- 95 documented business ideas with execution notes
- Scripts and automation included (real working code)
- Niche enough to avoid commodity pricing

## Validated?
No. EUR 499 is high for Gumroad (most products $29-99). The audience (people wanting EU recruitment data + ideas) is very niche.

## Price
EUR 499 one-shot. Alternative: EUR 99 for data-only, EUR 299 for data+ideas, EUR 499 for full bundle with scripts. Tiered pricing increases conversion.

## Risk
High. Very niche audience, hard to find buyers on Gumroad. EUR 499 is top-of-range for Gumroad products. Data freshness: buyers expect updates. Legal: selling scraped data may violate GDPR/terms.

## Recommendation
WAIT. Better to sell specific data products (e.g., "3,751 EU Factory Employers with Emails" for EUR 49) than one mega-bundle. Test with 3-5 smaller products first. If any sells 10+ copies, then consider the bundle.
