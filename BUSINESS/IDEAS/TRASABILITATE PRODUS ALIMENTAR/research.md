# Research: TRASABILITATE (IDEA-012)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Food traceability SaaS for Romanian producers selling to hypermarkets and EU export. FastAPI backend, QR-based batch tracking, compliance reports. Two tracks: A (packaged products, 26 warm leads) and B (loose produce, 60% built).

## Market Size
- Global food traceability software market: USD 291.5M in 2026, growing 7.45% CAGR
- Europe segment: USD 78.71M (27% of global) in 2026
- Broader food traceability market: USD 23.3B globally
- EU Farm to Fork Strategy driving mandatory traceability (EU Reg. 178/2002)
- New EU regulations from 2025 require enhanced traceability for imported food
- Romania-specific: zero local competitors identified

## Our Advantage
- Zero Romanian competitors (blank market, first-mover)
- 269 addressable producers from RNPM database (hypermarket suppliers, EU exporters, B2B distributors)
- Cooperative bundling (Gospodarii de Altadata) reduces per-producer cost
- Romanian language + local support = competitive moat
- Pre-integration with hypermarket requirements (Kaufland QA)
- 12-month window before international competitors localize

## Validated?
Partially. EU regulations mandate traceability. 26 warm leads identified from RNPM database. No customer interviews yet. Go/no-go depends on: (1) Kaufland procurement confirmation, (2) 2-3 producer beta tests.

## Price
- Starter: EUR 99/month (1 producer, <50 batches)
- Pro: EUR 299/month (5 producers, <500 batches)
- Enterprise: EUR 999+/month (unlimited, distributor)
- Baseline MRR: EUR 1,900 (12 producers at 50% adoption)
- Year 1 ARR target: EUR 26K

## Risk
- MEDIUM: No customer validation yet (need Kaufland + producer interviews)
- 60% of code built but no paying users
- Producers may resist software adoption (tech literacy)
- Price sensitivity: Romanian SMEs may find EUR 99/mo too high
- 12-month competitive window is short

## Recommendation
**GO Track B first, then A** - Finish the MVP (40% remaining), get 3 free beta users from RNPM contacts, validate with Kaufland procurement. If Kaufland confirms batch trace requirement, the market is real. EUR 99/mo is justified by EU compliance requirements. Pairs with Cooperativa (IDEA-011) and Produs Montan (IDEA-016) for bundled offering.
