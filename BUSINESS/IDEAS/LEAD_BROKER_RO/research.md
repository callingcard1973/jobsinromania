# Research: LEAD BROKER RO (IDEA-049)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Sell qualified employer leads to Romanian temporary staffing agencies. EUR 50-200/lead from existing campaign responses and database enrichment.

## Market Size & Demand
- B2B average cost per lead: $84-$198 globally (2025)
- Recruitment industry CPL: $200-$500+ for qualified leads
- B2B lead generation services: $2,500-$12,000+/month retainer
- Content/email CPL: $50-$100 for B2B companies
- Qualified SQL (Sales Qualified Lead): EUR 150-400 in UK recruitment market
- Global online recruitment tech market: $11.5B growing to $30.9B by 2030 (15.2% CAGR)
- Romania has 30+ major recruitment agencies (Adecco, Hays, Manpower, Lugera, Prohuman, etc.)

## Our Advantage
- Already generating employer leads from 56 active campaign sectors
- 2,665+ enriched employer contacts with verified emails
- Campaign responses route to manpower.dristor@gmail.com - can qualify and resell
- Deep knowledge of Romanian temporary work market (agencies, sectors, regions)

## Market Validated?
YES. Recruitment agencies routinely pay for leads. The 30+ agencies operating in Romania all need employer clients. Commission-based referrals are standard in the industry (15-25% of first placement fee). Lead selling at flat rate is less common in RO but standard globally.

## Price Point
- EUR 50/lead: Basic (company name + contact + sector + hiring need)
- EUR 100/lead: Qualified (verified hiring need + budget + timeline)
- EUR 200/lead: Hot (active response to campaign + confirmed interest in temporary workers)
- Subscription: EUR 500/month for 10 leads/month guaranteed

## Risk
- MEDIUM. Lead quality is everything - bad leads destroy reputation fast
- Legal: Must ensure GDPR compliance when reselling contact data
- Relationship risk: agencies may try to bypass you after first introduction
- Market size in Romania is limited (30-50 serious agencies)
- Need clear contracts with exclusivity periods

## Recommendation
GO with caution. Start with 3-5 trusted agencies on commission basis (no upfront cost to them). Track conversion rates. If >20% of leads convert to placements, switch to flat-rate pricing. Low investment since leads come from existing campaigns.
