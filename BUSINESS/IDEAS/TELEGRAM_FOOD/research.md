# Research: TELEGRAM FOOD (IDEA-008)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Telegram channel @FoodTendersRO publishing daily SEAP food/catering tenders. Monetize via sponsored posts from food suppliers wanting to win government contracts.

## Market Size & Demand
- SEAP handles 16.2B EUR in contracts annually (2023 estimate), 50K+ contracting authorities
- Food/catering is a significant SEAP category (schools, hospitals, military, prisons)
- No Telegram channels found specifically for Romanian food tenders (gap confirmed)
- Existing tender monitoring platforms are web-based, expensive, not mobile-first
- Romania has thousands of food producers/distributors competing for public contracts

## Competitors Found

| Competitor | Type | Pricing | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| licitatia.ro | Web + mobile app | Subscription (undisclosed) | General tenders, not food-specific |
| licitatii.app | Web platform | Subscription (undisclosed) | SEAP monitoring, market analysis |
| infocentrum.ro | Web portal | Free/paid tiers | SEAP aggregator |
| licitatiiseap.ro | Blog + guides | Free content | SEO-driven, informational |
| seap-licitatie.ro | Web platform | Unknown | Documentation services |
| tendersinfo.com | Global platform | Paid subscription | Romania as one country among many |
| globaltenders.com | Global platform | Paid subscription | Generic, not food-specific |

## Our Advantage
- ZERO competition on Telegram for RO food tenders (blue ocean)
- Automated: SEAP scraper already exists on raspibig, just filter by food CPV codes
- Instant delivery: Telegram push vs email/web login
- Low barrier: food suppliers check phones, not SaaS dashboards
- Can cross-sell to IDEA-005 (SEAP_FOOD) and IDEA-044 (Telegram bot)

## Market Validated?
PARTIALLY. Tender monitoring is a proven market (licitatia.ro, licitatii.app exist and charge subscriptions). Telegram as a channel for tenders is unvalidated in Romania but proven in other countries (Russia, Ukraine have active tender Telegram channels). Food suppliers actively search for SEAP opportunities.

## Price Point
- Sponsored posts: 50-200 EUR per post (food supplier promoting their bid capability)
- Premium tier: 20-50 EUR/month for advance alerts (24h before public posting)
- Estimated revenue: 500-2000 EUR/month with 50-100 subscribers
- Scale: add more sectors (construction, IT, medical) for 5-10x

## Risk
- LOW effort (1 hour setup, automated posting)
- MEDIUM monetization risk: Romanian food suppliers may not use Telegram heavily
- LOW downside: if it fails, only wasted 1 hour

## Recommendation
**LAUNCH** -- Minimal effort (1 hour), zero cost, automated via existing SEAP scraper. Start the channel, post daily for 30 days, measure subscriber growth. If >100 subscribers in month 1, add sponsored posts. Perfect quick-win test.
