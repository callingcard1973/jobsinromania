# Research: SEAP FOOD (IDEA-005)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Email campaign to 3,030 SEAP food procurement winners proposing cooperative supply partnerships. Target: companies that already won public food contracts (schools, hospitals, military) and need reliable suppliers.

## Market Size & Demand
- Romania total public procurement: ~EUR 15-20B/year (EU average ~14% of GDP)
- Food procurement (CPV codes 15xxxxxx) is significant subset — schools, hospitals, military, prisons
- 3,030 identified winners already in our enriched CSV
- SEAP processes thousands of food tenders annually — winners need ongoing supply chains
- Romanian food retail market: EUR 26B+ (2014 data, significantly higher by 2026)
- Public food procurement increasingly requires local/ecological sourcing (EU Green Deal pressure)
- Average food supply contract: EUR 50K-500K depending on institution size

| Name | Type | Focus |

| DataDriven.ro | SEAP analytics | Procurement data intelligence, tender monitoring |

**Note:** No competitor is doing what we propose — contacting WINNERS to offer supply partnerships. All competitors help companies FIND and WIN tenders. We flip the model: approach winners who need suppliers.

## Our Advantage
- **Unique angle**: Nobody emails winners offering to be their supplier — this is a blue ocean
- **3,030 enriched contacts ready** — names, emails, CUI, contract values from SEAP public data
- **Cooperative model**: Can aggregate supply from multiple small producers (OIPA/AgroEvolution network)
- **AgroEvolution.com already live** — credible agricultural platform for credibility
- **MADR land data** (9,658 listings) + farmer contacts complement food supply pitch
- **Low effort**: 2 hours to launch — template + CSV + existing email infrastructure

## Market Validated? YES
- SEAP winners are publicly listed — confirmed they have active contracts needing fulfillment
- Public institutions MUST buy food (schools, hospitals) — demand is non-discretionary
- Multiple tender monitoring platforms exist (DataDriven, LicitatiiSEAP) proving market interest
- EU pushing for local/short food supply chains in public procurement (Green Deal)
- Winners often struggle to source quality/quantity — cooperative solves this

## Price Point
- Not a subscription — this is B2B supply partnership
- Revenue per contract: EUR 500-5,000 commission/margin on supply deals
- Or: cooperative membership fee from producers + markup on sales to institutions
- Target: 10 active partnerships = EUR 5K-50K/year
- Scale potential: position as food procurement intermediary across Romania

## Risk
- **Low response rate** — food companies may already have established suppliers
- **Logistics complexity** — actually delivering food to institutions requires cold chain, HACCP
- **Thin margins** — food supply margins are 5-15%, need volume
- **Seasonal variability** — some products unavailable year-round
- **Contract obligations** — SEAP winners have strict delivery schedules and quality requirements
- **Trust**: winners may not trust unknown email sender for food supply

## Recommendation: LAUNCH
Zero cost campaign (2h setup), unique positioning (nobody else contacts winners), synergy with AgroEvolution/OIPA/MADR assets. Even if only 1% respond (30 companies), a few partnerships can generate EUR 5K-50K/year. The data is already enriched and ready.
