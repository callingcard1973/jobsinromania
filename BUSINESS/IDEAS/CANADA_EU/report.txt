5. DELAWARE COMPANY FORMATION FOR COOPERATIVES
================================================================================

WHY DELAWARE FOR COOPERATIVES:

Advantage               Description
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Privacy                 No public disclosure of member names
Court of Chancery       Expert business court, fast resolution
Pass-through taxation   No double taxation for non-US members
No state income tax     For out-of-state operations
No citizenship req      International members allowed
Flexible governance     "Freedom of contract" principle

DELAWARE WORKERS COOPERATIVE ACT (Title 6, Chapter 14):

Delaware has specific legislation for worker cooperatives:
- Majority board elected by members (1 member, 1 vote)
- Majority of employees must be members
- Majority of members must be employees
- Can be organized for any lawful business

RECOMMENDED STRUCTURE FOR TUDOR'S 140-MEMBER COOPERATIVE:

Delaware LLC (Master Holding)
    |
    +-- Romanian Agricultural Cooperative (operations)
    |
    +-- Canadian Export Corp (CETA/CPTPP access)
