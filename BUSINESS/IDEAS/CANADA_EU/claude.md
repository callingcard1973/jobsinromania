## 5. Delaware Company Formation for Cooperatives

### 5.1 Why Delaware for Cooperatives

Delaware offers unique advantages for international cooperative structures:

| Advantage | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Privacy** | No public disclosure of member names |
| **Court of Chancery** | Expert business court, fast resolution |
| **Pass-through taxation** | No double taxation for non-US members |
| **No state income tax** | For out-of-state operations |
| **No citizenship requirement** | International members allowed |
| **Flexible governance** | "Freedom of contract" principle |

### 5.2 Delaware Workers Cooperative Act (Title 6, Chapter 14)

Delaware has specific legislation for worker cooperatives:

- **Majority board elected** by members (1 member, 1 vote)
- **Majority of employees** must be members
- **Majority of members** must be employees
- Can be organized for **any lawful business**

### 5.3 LLC Structure for Agricultural Cooperative

**Recommended structure for Tudor's 140-member agricultural cooperative:**

```
Delaware LLC (Master Holding)
├── Romanian Agricultural Cooperative (operations)
├── Canadian Export Corp (CETA/CPTPP access)
└── EU Distribution Network (via Romania)
```

**Formation Process:**
1. File Certificate of Formation - $90
2. Designate registered agent (Delaware address)
3. Create Operating Agreement with cooperative governance
4. Annual franchise tax - $300
5. Corporate Transparency Act filing (beneficial owners to FinCEN)

### 5.4 Tax Considerations

| Scenario | Tax Treatment |
|----------|---------------|
| No US operations | No US income tax (only $300/yr franchise tax) |
| US-generated income | Pass-through to members' tax returns |
| Non-US members | May need to file US returns for US income |
| Romania | Check DTT treatment (Canada-Romania DTA exists) |

### 5.5 Operating Agreement Elements for Cooperative LLC

Essential provisions:
- **Patronage dividends** distribution based on member participation
- **One member, one vote** governance
- **Capital contribution requirements** and returns
- **Member admission/withdrawal procedures**
- **Democratic decision-making processes**
- **Profit distribution formula** (participation-based, not capital-based)

### 5.6 Cost Estimate

| Item | Cost |
|------|------|

| Operating agreement (lawyer) | $2,000-5,000 |
