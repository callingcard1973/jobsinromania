# Research: NORWEGIAN JOBS NEWSLETTER (IDEA-104)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Substack newsletter "Norwegian Jobs Weekly" - top 10 NO jobs for Romanians. Free tier builds audience, monetize with premium listings EUR 50/post and sponsored slots.

## Market Size
- Substack: 5M+ paid subscribers (March 2025), 50+ creators earning $1M+/year
- Niche job newsletters: proven model (The Bear Cave: $300K/yr from scratch)
- Romanian diaspora Norway: ~10,000 workers, growing demand
- 1,000 paid subscribers x $5/mo = $60K/yr (Substack benchmark)

## Competitors
| Competitor | Type | Price | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|-------|
| EURES Job Alerts | Email alerts | Free | EU-wide, not Norway-specific |
| NAV Arbeidsplassen | Job alerts | Free | Norwegian government, generic |
| LinkedIn Jobs Norway | Alerts | Free/Premium | Generic, not RO-targeted |
| Facebook groups RO-NO | Community | Free | Unstructured, spammy |
| No direct Substack competitor | - | - | Gap in market |

## Our Advantage
- No existing "Norwegian Jobs for Romanians" newsletter
- Already have buildjobs.eu/no/ pages with job content
- Email infrastructure to promote newsletter (6,300/day)
- Can auto-generate from scraped FINN.no/NAV data
- Cross-sell: newsletter readers become IDEA-099 applicants and IDEA-111 CV database entries

## Validated?
Not yet, but model is proven. Substack job newsletters in other niches work. RO->NO corridor has no newsletter.

## Price
- Free tier: weekly top 10 jobs
- Paid: EUR 5/mo for daily alerts + exclusive listings
- Sponsored job posts: EUR 50-100/post (employers pay)
- Revenue estimate: 500 free + 100 paid = EUR 6,000/yr subscriptions + EUR 12,000/yr sponsored = EUR 18,000/yr

## Risk
Low. Near-zero cost (Substack is free). Time investment ~2h/week. Romanian audience may not pay for Substack (price sensitivity). Better monetize via sponsored posts than subscriptions.

## Recommendation
GO. Start immediately, zero cost. Publish weekly, curate from FINN.no + NAV. Build to 1,000 subscribers in 6 months. Monetize via sponsored employer posts (EUR 50-100 each). Feed into IDEA-099 and IDEA-111.
