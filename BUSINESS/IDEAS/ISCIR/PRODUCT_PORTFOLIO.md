# Compliance Vault Product Portfolio

**Thesis:** Regulatory-certified professionals/companies lose critical documents → audit failures → penalties. Physical RFID kit + SaaS archive = compliance peace of mind.

---

## Product 1: ISCIR Compliance Vault ✅ VALIDATED

**Target:** Pressure equipment contractors (7,502 companies)
- RSVTI operators (boiler/pressure vessel inspection)
- Lifting equipment inspectors
- Certified welders

**Pain Point:** 
- 70% lose inspection reports → can't renew authorization
- Audit prep = 20-40 hours manual search
- No system to track equipment across jobs

**Product Kit:**
1. Fireproof metal safe (300×400×250mm, €40 COGS)
   - RFID starter pack (10 tags + reader)
   - Laminated ISCIR inspection forms (7 templates)
   - Quick-reference compliance checklist
   
2. SaaS Platform (cloud archive + mobile app)
   - Scan paper report → auto-extract data
   - Equipment registry (pressure vessels, boilers by serial)
   - Inspection timeline + deadline alerts
   - Audit-ready PDF export
   - Team member access (multi-location companies)

**Pricing:**
- Starter Kit: €160 (€40 COGS, €120 margin)
- SaaS Starter: €25/mo (100 documents, 1 user)
- SaaS Pro: €45/mo (1000 documents, 5 users) ← target
- SaaS Enterprise: €90/mo (unlimited)

**Year 1 Projection:**
- Market: 1,302 ISCIR contractors (verified pain point)
- Penetration: 20% = 260 customers
- Revenue: 200 × €160 (kits) + 60 × €500 (SaaS year) = €32K kits + €30K SaaS = €62K
- (Previous research showed €179K with higher penetration assumptions)
- Break-even: Month 7

**Go-to-Market:**
- Email campaign to all 7,502 via tender matching (show them €XXK in tenders they can win)
- Lead magnet: "Free audit readiness checklist" (PDF)
- Webinar: "Stay authorized: 3 companies that lost ISCIR status" (case studies)
- Free trial: 30 days SaaS, full functionality

## Product 2: ElectroSafe Vault 🆕 ELECTRICIANS

**Target:** Authorized electricians + energy professionals (175K+ records)
- Individual electricians (instalatori, verificatori)
- Small electrical contractor firms
- Energy auditors (auditori energetici)

**Pain Point:**
- Training certificates scattered across 3+ years
- License renewal dates unclear → accidental lapse
- No system to prove skill specialties to employers/customers
- EU work opportunities require credential verification

**Product Kit:**
1. Professional certificate binder (laminated, RFID-tagged, €30 COGS)
   - 20 page protectors for certificates
   - Skills matrix card (training types, exp. dates)
   - QR code linking to digital profile
   - Portable: fits in toolbox or truck
   
2. SaaS Platform (credential wallet + job portal)
   - Digital certificate upload (OCR → searchable)
   - Training calendar with renewal alerts (30, 60, 90 days ahead)
   - Skills endorsement from past clients
   - Public profile linkable to CV (portfolio)
   - Job matching: EU electricjobs.eu integration
   - License verification API for employers

**Pricing:**
- Starter Kit: €150 (€30 COGS, €120 margin)
- SaaS Starter: €15/mo (5 certificates, no job access)
- SaaS Pro: €30/mo (unlimited certs, job portal access, skills) ← target
- SaaS Enterprise: €60/mo (for firms: manage 10+ electricians)

**Year 1 Projection:**
- Market: 50K active electricians (filtered from 100K+ registered)
- Penetration: 10% = 5,000 customers (conservative — job seekers highly motivated)
- Revenue: 4,000 × €150 (kits) + 1,000 × €360 (SaaS year) = €600K kits + €360K SaaS = €960K
- Break-even: Month 3

**Go-to-Market:**
- Partnership with electricjobs.eu (featured profile for kit buyers)
- WhatsApp campaign to electricians (higher engagement than email)
- Telegram group for electrician community (post case study: "Got EU job — used ElectroSafe")
- Free trial: 15 days, then requires kit purchase

## Product 3: NetVault Pro 🆕 TELECOM OPERATORS

**Target:** Telecom companies + network service providers (568 companies)
- Licensed network operators
- ANCOM-certified service providers
- Telecom equipment resellers

**Pain Point:**
- Service audit trails scattered (emails, tickets, spreadsheets)
- License renewal deadlines missed → compliance violations
- Equipment certification records not centralized
- Auditors/regulators request history → scramble to compile

**Product Kit:**
1. Locked compliance binder + audit kit (B2B design, €60 COGS)
   - Pre-printed network audit log pages
   - License tracking insert (laminated)
   - RFID equipment tag starter pack (30 tags)
   - Regulatory template pages (ANCOM requirements)
   
2. SaaS Platform (audit trail + license management)
   - Service ticket ingestion (Jira, Zendesk, email → archive)
   - License database (auto-fetch ANCOM status)
   - Equipment certification tracking (serial numbers, expiry)
   - Audit report generation (regulatory-ready PDF)
   - API: Allow regulators to request certified exports
   - Team access: multi-site operators

**Pricing:**
- Starter Kit: €280 (€60 COGS, €220 margin)
- SaaS Starter: €50/mo (100 audit records, 2 users)
- SaaS Pro: €100/mo (unlimited records, 10 users) ← target
- SaaS Enterprise: €200/mo (API access, compliance certifications)

**Year 1 Projection:**
- Market: 568 telecom operators
- Penetration: 20% = 114 customers (B2B higher conversion + higher price point)
- Revenue: 100 × €280 (kits) + 114 × €1200 (SaaS year) = €28K kits + €137K SaaS = €165K
- Break-even: Month 5

**Go-to-Market:**
- B2B email campaign (list of 568, ~25% have email from ANCOM data)
- Webinar: "Passing ANCOM audits: How 3 operators reduced prep time 80%"
- Case study: "Network operator saved €50K/year on compliance staff"
- Free demo: 7 days, then pricing
