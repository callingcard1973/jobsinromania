# Research: ALUMINUM (IDEA-035)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Use 611 aluminum/recycling companies extracted from TED procurement data (with emails) + aluminumrecyclehub.com domain to recruit workers for European recycling plants.

## Market Size & Demand
- European aluminum scrap market: USD 2.4B in 2024, growing at 6.5% CAGR to 2031
- Global aluminum recycling market: USD 57.2B (2024), projected USD 91.3B by 2032
- EU Circular Economy Action Plan targets 65% aluminum scrap recycling by 2035
- Norsk Hydro investing EUR 180M in new Spanish recycling plant (2025) — expansion = hiring
- Major players: Novelis, Norsk Hydro, Real Alloy, Constellium, Stena Metall
- Sector growing due to EV adoption, construction demand, and EU green policies

## Competitors Found

| Competitor | What | Pricing | Threat |
|------------|------|---------|--------|
| Europages (recycling agencies) | General industrial recruitment listings | Listing fees | Low — directory, not active recruitment |
| Adecco/Randstad/Manpower | General staffing inc. recycling/manufacturing | 15-25% margin | HIGH — established, trusted brands |
| Local temp agencies (per country) | Country-specific blue-collar placement | Per-worker fee | Medium — fragmented, no pan-European reach |
| Personalvermittlung.eu | Eastern European workers for industry | Per-worker fee | Medium — not recycling-specific |

## Our Advantage
- 611 verified companies with emails from TED procurement (decision-makers, not HR generalists)
- aluminumrecyclehub.com domain — ready for niche authority site
- Existing worker pipeline for blue-collar/manufacturing roles
- Email campaign infrastructure (2,500+/day)
- Low competition in recycling-specific recruitment niche
- Only 5 hours estimated effort to launch campaign

## Market Validated?
PARTIALLY — Aluminum recycling is growing fast and needs workers (new plants being built). However, no evidence of recycling-specific recruitment agencies. Workers for these roles are typically sourced through general temp agencies. The niche may be too narrow for a dedicated service.

## Price Point
- Worker placement fee: EUR 500-2,000 per worker (blue-collar manufacturing rates)
- Temp staffing margin: 15-25% of hourly rate
- 611 companies, even 2% conversion = 12 clients
- 12 clients x 5 workers x EUR 1,000 = EUR 60K revenue potential

## Risk
- LOW-MEDIUM: Small database (611 companies), but very low effort to test (5 hours)
- Recycling jobs are often low-wage, reducing placement fees
- Companies may already have temp agency contracts
- Geographic spread across Europe complicates logistics

## Recommendation
**LAUNCH** — Minimal effort (5 hours), data already exists, domain ready. Send targeted email campaign to 611 companies offering Romanian workers for recycling/manufacturing roles. Even modest conversion (2-3%) generates meaningful revenue. Use aluminumrecyclehub.com as landing page. If no response after one campaign cycle, PARK it.
