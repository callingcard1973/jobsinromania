# Research: DOCUMENT PREPARATION (IDEA-086)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Pregatire documente: permise munca + vize + contracte + detasare A1. EUR 100-300/dosar. Automatizat cu template-uri.

## Market Size
- Romania work permit applications: growing yearly (non-EU workers increasing)
- Government fees: RON 100 (single permit), RON 500 (EU Blue Card), EUR 120 (D-type visa)
- Processing time: 30-45 days per application
- Immigration law firms charge EUR 500-2000+ per case
- Estimated 50K+ work permits issued/year in Romania

## Our Advantage
- Template automation: 80% of documents are repetitive (contracts, A1 forms)
- Already know the process from placement campaigns
- Can bundle with recruitment (worker placement + docs = one-stop)
- LLM-assisted document drafting reduces manual work
- EUR 100-300 undercuts lawyers at EUR 500-2000+

## Market Validated?
YES. Multiple law firms and consultancies charge EUR 500-2000+ per case. EOR platforms charge $300-600/mo. Our EUR 100-300 is significantly cheaper via automation.

## Price Point
- Basic (A1 form only): EUR 100/dosar
- Standard (work permit + visa docs): EUR 200/dosar
- Premium (full package: permit + visa + contract + A1): EUR 300/dosar
- Bulk: EUR 150/dosar for 20+ workers
- 50 dosare/mo x EUR 200 = EUR 10,000/mo

## Risk
- Legal liability: incorrect documents can delay/block workers
- May need partnership with licensed lawyer for certain filings
- Regulatory changes (immigration law updates frequently)
- Quality control: templates must stay current with law changes
- Client expectations: they want guaranteed approval

## Recommendation
STRONG GO. High synergy with recruitment business. Templates reduce cost to EUR 100-300 vs lawyer EUR 500-2000. Bundle with placement for one-stop service. Need legal review partnership.
