# Research: TED CONTACTE (IDEA-009)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Email campaign to 13,248 TED (Tenders Electronic Daily) procurement contacts offering Romanian workers at 30-40% labor cost savings vs Western European rates.

## Market Size & Demand
- TED publishes 3,000+ procurement notices daily, ~815B EUR in contracts annually
- European staffing market: 233B USD (2023), projected 687B USD by 2031 (14.5% CAGR)
- Romania has 701 temporary staffing agencies (growing 5.7% CAGR)
- Romanian worker wages are 3-5x lower than DE/FR/NL -- the 30-40% savings pitch is real
- Construction, manufacturing, logistics are top sectors for cross-border staffing

## Competitors Found

| Competitor | Type | Scale | Notes |

| Local agencies (hundreds) | Small RO agencies | 10-50 workers each | Fragmented market |

## Our Advantage
- 13,248 verified TED contacts already in PostgreSQL (competitors don't have this list)
- Direct outreach to procurement decision-makers (not HR, not job boards)
- Price angle is compelling: 30-40% savings on labor costs is concrete
- Already have email infrastructure: 1,560-2,560 emails/day capacity
- EBRD system adds 134 contractor contacts for infrastructure projects
- Can target by country, sector, contract value (TED data is structured)

## Market Validated?
YES. Cross-border staffing from Romania is a multi-billion EUR industry. Hundreds of Romanian agencies already do this. The innovation here is the outreach method (direct to TED procurement contacts) not the service itself.

## Price Point
- Placement fee: 500-2,000 EUR per worker placed (industry standard)
- Monthly margin: 200-500 EUR per active worker (ongoing contracts)
- At 13,248 contacts, even 0.5% conversion = 66 clients
- 66 clients x 10 workers x 500 EUR = 330,000 EUR in placement fees
- Conservative estimate: 50,000-150,000 EUR first year

## Risk
- MEDIUM: Cold email to procurement contacts may have low response rate (<1%)
- LOW cost: email campaign costs near zero (own infrastructure)
- MEDIUM legal: some countries restrict unsolicited B2B email (GDPR Art.6 legitimate interest applies for B2B)
- HIGH reward potential: even a few contracts = significant revenue

## Recommendation
**LAUNCH** -- Data is ready (13,248 contacts), infrastructure is ready (email campaigns live), service is proven (RO workers abroad is established market). This is the highest-revenue idea in the portfolio. Start with a test batch of 500 contacts in construction sector, measure response rate, then scale.
