## Slide 1: What We Offer
- Curated Romanian product assortment built from verified regional and mountain-product suppliers.
- First commercial focus on shelf-stable categories for easier onboarding.
- Structured for wholesale-first validation.

## Slide 2: Why This Model Works
- Reduces buyer friction created by fragmented small-producer supply.
- Provides one commercial path for assortment review, documentation, and invoice flow.
- Starts with easy-to-handle SKUs and expands once validated.
- Positions a registered cooperative as the buyer-facing aggregation layer.

## Slide 3: First Product Categories
- Honey and bee products.
- Preserves and jams.
- Natural juices and vinegar.
- Berry and dried-fruit products.
- Future expansion into dairy and cheese after logistics validation.

## Slide 4: Supply Base
- Seller sourcing comes from the existing `PRODUSMONTAN` pipeline and related producer materials.
- The first commercial pack prioritizes a small set of reliable sellers.
- Additional producer depth exists for later assortment expansion.

## Slide 5: Buyer Fit
- Metro Romania.
- Selgros Romania.
- La Doi Pasi via Metro.
- Regional wholesale and foodservice channels.
- Retail-chain local sourcing after pilot validation.
