# Research: NORWAY AGENCIES JOBFAIR (IDEA-096)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Filter NACE 78.xx (staffing agencies) from 314K Norwegian companies. Invite agencies to recruitment fairs in Romania (Bucharest/Hunedoara). 10 agencies x 30 placements x EUR 500-1,000 = EUR 15K-30K/fair.

## Market Size
- Norway staffing market: ~NOK 30B/year (~EUR 2.7B)
- Major players: Manpower, Adecco, Randstad, People4you, Dagsen
- EURES European Job Days already run cross-border fairs (free, EU-funded)
- Romanian workers in Norway: ~10,000+ (construction, hospitality, logistics)

## Competitors
| Competitor | Type | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| EURES European Job Days | Free, EU-funded | Multi-country, government-backed, limited frequency |
| Manpower Norway | Agency | Largest staffing firm, own recruitment events |
| Adecco Norway | Agency | Global brand, own pipeline |
| Airswift | Agency | 20+ years in Norway, oil/gas/construction |
| GoBester | Agency | IT focus, European + non-European workers |
| AJETS | Agency | Nepal/India to Norway pipeline |

## Our Advantage
- 314K Norwegian company database (can filter NACE 78.xx precisely)
- Existing email infra (6,300/day) to reach agencies directly
- Already have buildjobs.eu/no/ pages LIVE (5 Norway construction pages)
- Know Romanian worker supply side (AJOFM data, 8,037+ records)
- EURES fairs are rare (2-3/year); we can do monthly

## Validated?
Partially. EURES proves demand exists. Private job fairs for specific corridors (RO->NO) are uncommon = opportunity.

## Price
EUR 500-2,000/stand for agencies. Commission EUR 500-1,000/placement on top. Blended model.

## Risk
Medium-High. Norwegian agencies may prefer EURES (free) or their own channels. Need to prove Romanian worker quality/availability. Physical fair logistics if in-person (venue, travel).

## Recommendation
CONDITIONAL GO. Start virtual (IDEA-092 infra), not physical. Pilot with 20 NACE 78.xx agencies via email campaign. If 3+ respond, organize first virtual fair. Physical fairs only after proven demand.
