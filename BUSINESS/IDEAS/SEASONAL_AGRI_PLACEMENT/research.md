# Research: SEASONAL AGRI PLACEMENT (IDEA-080)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Plasare sezoniera recoltare vara (Jun-Sep). 1507 producatori montani. Candidati pe 15 domenii. EUR 200-500/plasare.

## Market Size
- EURES "Seize the Summer 2026": EU-wide seasonal recruitment (Apr-Oct)
- Sectors: agriculture, horticulture, food processing, harvest, vineyards
- Romania is major source country for seasonal workers to NL, DE, DK, NO, BE
- Estimated 200K+ Romanian seasonal workers/year in EU agriculture

## Our Advantage
- 1,507 mountain producers identified (MADR data)
- 15 recruitment domains with candidate pools
- Existing email campaign infrastructure (6,300/day)
- farmworkers.eu domain already owned
- EURES is free but bureaucratic; private placement is faster

## Market Validated?
YES. Agrigarant and others prove commercial viability. EURES 2026 seasonal campaign confirms massive demand. Standard agency fee: EUR 200-800/placement.

## Price Point
- Per placement: EUR 200-500 (standard market rate)
- Bulk deal: EUR 150/worker for 20+ placements
- Season package: EUR 3,000 for guaranteed 10 workers
- Revenue potential: 100 placements x EUR 300 = EUR 30,000/season

## Risk
- Seasonal (Jun-Sep only = 4 months revenue)
- Worker no-shows and early departures
- Competition from established agencies with farmer relationships
- Transportation/logistics coordination needed
- Labor law compliance across multiple countries

## Recommendation
GO for summer 2026. Low setup effort (5h). Use farmworkers.eu + existing candidate DB. Campaign to 1,507 MADR producers. Time-sensitive: must start by May.
