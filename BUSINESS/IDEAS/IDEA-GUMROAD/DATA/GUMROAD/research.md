# Research: GUMROAD DATA SALES (IDEA-001)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Sell 5 European business data CSV packages on Gumroad and alternatives: RO construction, TED procurement winners, Norway employers, food industry, recruitment agencies. 1.36M records across 16 countries.

## Market Size
- B2B data market dominated by ZoomInfo ($15K+/yr), Apollo ($59/mo), Lusha ($36/mo), Cognism (EMEA-focused)
- Gumroad hosts 250K+ digital products; data/spreadsheet products are a growing category
- European company data is underserved vs US data (Cognism has 250%+ more EU contacts than US competitors)
- Niche country-specific datasets sell for $29-$199 on Gumroad/Payhip

## Competitors
| Competitor | What | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|------|-------|----------|
| ZoomInfo | B2B contact database | $15,000+/yr | Expensive, US-focused, no niche EU data |
| Apollo.io | Sales intelligence platform | $59-149/mo | Weak Eastern Europe coverage |
| Lusha | Contact enrichment | $36-79/mo | Limited Romania/Balkans data |
| Cognism | EMEA B2B data | Custom (high) | Enterprise pricing, no one-off CSV |
| Dealfront | EU company data | Custom | No self-serve CSV downloads |
| UpLead | Verified B2B contacts | $99/mo | Thin EU coverage |
| Gumroad data sellers | Various CSV datasets | $9-99 | Generic, not country-specific |

## Our Advantage
- 1.36M records already cleaned and structured across 16 EU countries
- Niche datasets nobody else has (TED winners, ANOFM sectors, ISC construction)
- Self-serve pricing ($19-$199) fills gap between free and enterprise ($15K+)
- Multiple platforms: Gumroad (10%), Payhip (5%), LemonSqueezy (5%)
- Zero marginal cost per sale

## Validated?
Partially. Gumroad data products sell (GumTrends shows active market). EU company data is in demand (Cognism raised $150M+). No direct sales validation yet. GDPR compliance needed for contact data.

## Price
- Sample: Free (100 records)
- Starter: $19 (1,000 records)
- Pro: $49 (5,000 records)
- Enterprise: $29-199 (full country dataset)

## Risk
- MEDIUM: GDPR compliance is critical - selling email/phone data requires legitimate interest or consent basis
- Gumroad takes 10% + currency conversion fees for EU sellers
- Data freshness decays (need update mechanism)
- Potential takedown requests from data subjects

## Recommendation
**GO with caution** - Start with company-level data only (name, CUI, CAEN, county, website) which is public record. Avoid selling personal emails/phones directly. TED procurement data and company registries are safest. Test with 3 products on Payhip (lower fees) first.
