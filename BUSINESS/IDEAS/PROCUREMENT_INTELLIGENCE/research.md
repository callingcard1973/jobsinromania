# Research: PROCUREMENT INTELLIGENCE (IDEA-124)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Weekly newsletter with curated tenders by sector (construction, IT, food, medical). Automated from TED+SEAP+EBRD. EUR 99/month subscription.
