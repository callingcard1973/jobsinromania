# Research: RECRUITMENT LICENSE BROKER (IDEA-118)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Prepare ANOFM/Ministry of Labor dossiers for recruitment agencies seeking temporary work agent authorization. EUR 500-1000/dossier. Target: new agencies, foreign companies entering Romania.

## Market Size
- ~500 new recruitment agencies registered in Romania per year (ITM data)
- License valid 2 years, must be renewed = recurring clients
- Romania has ~2,000 active temporary work agents
- Adjacent: EURES cross-border placement licenses, ANOFM mediation authorizations

## Our Advantage
- Already know the recruitment industry (28 websites, campaigns, ANOFM data)
- Can bundle with employer database access, campaign services

## Validated?
Partially. Requirements well-documented (HG 1256/2011, Labor Code art. 88-102). Process takes 30 days. Real demand from foreign agencies wanting Romania presence. Low barrier to offer.

## Price
EUR 500-1000/dossier. Premium package with contacts: EUR 1500. Renewal: EUR 300.

## Risk
- LOW: Bureaucratic service, no liability if license denied (refund policy)
- Legal gray area: must frame as "document preparation" not "legal advice"
- Small market: 20-50 clients/year realistic = EUR 10K-25K/year

## Recommendation
GO as add-on service, not standalone. Bundle with recruitment website setup + employer database. Low effort, good margin, complements existing business.
