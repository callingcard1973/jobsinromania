# Research: SELL NGO LEADS (IDEA-057)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Sell scored/enriched list of 5,000 Romanian NGOs to marketing agencies, NGO consultants, and IT service providers. EUR 50-200/list.

## Market Size & Demand
- Romania has 142,128 registered nonprofits (GlobalGiving Atlas)
- NGO mailing list pricing: $100-$250 CPM (cost per thousand) for email lists
- Data append services: $0.10-$0.40 per record (email append)
- BizProspex NGO email list: starting at $200 for basic list
- Consumer postal lists: $40-$150 CPM; email lists higher
- Bulk discounts common for 5K+ records
- Buyers: marketing agencies, EU funds consultants, IT vendors, training providers

## Competitors Found
| Competitor | Focus | Pricing | Coverage |
|-----------|-------|---------|----------|
| GlobalGiving Atlas | NGO directory + API | Free download (basic) | 142K RO NGOs |
| NGO Base (ngobase.org) | Global NGO directory | Free browsable | Romania + global |
| Lusha | B2B contact data | $29-79/mo/user | Romania NGOs available |
| WANGO Directory | Global NGO directory | Free searchable | 54K+ orgs, 190 countries |
| Bancomail | B2B email lists by sector | EUR 100+ minimum order | Romania available |
| BizProspex | NGO email lists | From $200 | Global |
| InfoGlobalData | Nonprofit mailing lists | Custom quote | Global |

## Our Advantage
- 5,000 NGOs already scored and enriched (not just raw registry data)
- Scoring adds value over free directories (activity level, funding, sector)
- Romanian-specific focus with local knowledge
- Can segment by sector, county, budget, activity status
- Enrichment includes verified emails, phones, social media
- Competitors offer raw data; we offer actionable intelligence

## Market Validated?
PARTIALLY. Free NGO directories exist (GlobalGiving, NGO Base) which limits willingness to pay for basic lists. However, ENRICHED and SCORED lists with verified contacts command premium. Data brokers like Bancomail and BizProspex confirm market exists for paid lists.

## Price Point
- EUR 50: Basic list (name + CUI + address + sector, 5K records)
- EUR 150: Enriched list (+ verified email + phone + activity score)
- EUR 200: Premium (+ social media + funding history + EU projects received)
- Recurring: EUR 30/month for quarterly updates

## Risk
- HIGH. Free alternatives exist (GlobalGiving offers CSV download of 142K RO NGOs)
- GDPR: Selling personal contact data of NGO staff requires legal basis
- Small market: limited number of buyers interested in Romanian NGO data specifically
- One-time purchase model means low LTV per customer
- Price sensitivity: EUR 50-200 is very low revenue per transaction

## Recommendation
SKIP or BUNDLE ONLY. Free NGO directories undercut standalone list sales. The 5K scored list has more value as a lead-gen tool for your own campaigns (e.g., selling IT services, EU funds consulting) than as a product to sell. If pursued, bundle with other data products (e.g., company reports, insolvency data) rather than selling standalone.
