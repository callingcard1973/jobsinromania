### **Market Risks**
- **Italian companies may not see value in Norwegian data**
  - *Mitigation*: Start with energy sector (proven high demand)
  
- **Competition from established market research firms**
  - *Mitigation*: Focus on exclusive Norwegian data niche

### **Operational Risks**
- **Data quality issues**
  - *Mitigation*: Verification and enrichment services included
  
- **Payment collection from international clients**
  - *Mitigation*: Use international payment processors, require upfront deposits

### **Regulatory Risks**
- **GDPR compliance for cross-border data transfer**
