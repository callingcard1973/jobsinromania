## Our Advantage
- **4,175 verified phone numbers** from SEAP food procurement -- unique dataset
- **90% open rate** vs 20-30% email -- dramatically better reach
- **Niche targeting**: food procurement officers at Romanian public institutions
- **Content ready**: OIPA cooperative, Delecroix, agri products for promotion

## Market Validated?
YES for WhatsApp B2B messaging (Twilio, WATI, Respond.io profitable). UNTESTED for Romanian public procurement food sector. Channel proven; niche unproven but logical.

## Price Point
- Cost to send: ~$0.03-0.08 per marketing message (Romania estimated)
- 4,175 contacts x $0.05 = ~$209 per blast
- Monthly: $209-627 (1-3 blasts) + BSP fee ($49-79/mo)
- Total: ~EUR 300-700/month
- Revenue per conversion: EUR 500-5,000 per food supply contract

## Risk
- HIGH -- WhatsApp strict anti-spam policies
- Sending to 4,175 without opt-in = account ban
- WhatsApp requires Facebook Business verification
- Public procurement officers may not respond to WhatsApp marketing
- GDPR: phone numbers from SEAP for marketing is legally gray
- Meta can terminate API access for spam complaints
- Cost per message adds up vs free email

## Recommendation
PROCEED WITH CAUTION. Do NOT blast 4,175 at once -- instant ban. Instead: (1) Get WhatsApp Business API via Twilio/WATI, (2) Start with 50 warm leads (existing email respondents), (3) Use utility messages (tender alerts, not marketing) to build opt-in, (4) Scale to 200-500/week gradually. Consider combining with IDEA-044 (tender alerts via WhatsApp for food sector).
