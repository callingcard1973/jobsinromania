# Research: NEWSLETTER SPONSORSHIP (IDEA-071)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
6,300 emailuri/zi capacity. Vinde spatiu sponsorizat in campaniile existente. EUR 50-200/1000 emailuri. Niche B2B = CPM mare.

## Market Size
- Newsletter advertising: growing segment of $500B+ digital ad market
- B2B newsletter CPMs: $50-150 (vs $5-15 display)
- Email ROI: $36-42 per $1 spent (highest channel)

## Our Advantage
- 6,300 emails/day = ~189K/month = real scale
- B2B recruitment niche = premium CPMs ($50-120)
- Audience: European employers actively hiring = high commercial intent
- Zero additional sending cost (sponsors ride existing campaigns)
- Can segment by country, sector, company size

## Validated?
YES -- Newsletter sponsorship is proven, growing market. B2B niche commands $50-150 CPM.

## Price
Conservative: $50 CPM x 189K = $9,450/mo potential. Realistic (30% fill): $2,835/mo.

## Risk
- MEDIUM: May hurt deliverability if ads seen as spam
- Cold emails + ads = reduced response rates
- Need advertiser sales pipeline
- CAN-SPAM/GDPR gray area for sponsored content in cold emails

## Recommendation
TEST CAREFULLY -- Add small "Powered by [Sponsor]" to ONE segment (100/day). Measure open/reply impact. If no drop, approach HR SaaS companies. Do NOT add to cold recruitment emails.
