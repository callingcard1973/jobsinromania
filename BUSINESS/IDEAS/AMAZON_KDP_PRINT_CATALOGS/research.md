# Research: AMAZON KDP PRINT CATALOGS (IDEA-136)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Print-on-demand catalogs on Amazon KDP: EU employers by sector, construction companies, EBRD projects, agricultural buyers. EUR 15-49/book. Passive income after publication.

## Market Size
- Amazon: ~300M printed books/year, ~50% of US print book market
- KDP systematic publishers earn $3K-10K/month with 5-10 titles/month
- Amazon book revenue: ~$28B/year globally
- Niche directory/data books: small but steady (business directories, trade catalogs)
- Low-content/data books: growing, less saturated in non-English markets

## Competitors
| Competitor | Type | Price | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|-------|
| Generic KDP publishers | Data books | $10-30 | Address books, logs, planners |
| Kompass directories | Print directories | EUR 50-200 | Traditional B2B, declining |
| D&B/Hoovers | Company directories | $100+ | Enterprise, not consumer |
| Europages | Online directory | Free/premium | No print product |
| Niche KDP operators | Systematic | $15-49 | Volume strategy, 5-10/month |
| Book Bolt users | KDP tools | $9.99/mo tool | Research + design |

## Our Advantage
- Data exists: 2,665+ employers, 208M companies in DB
- HTML/PDF pipeline ready (generate_catalog_master.py + Playwright)
- 11-language capability for pan-European editions
- Zero competition in niche: "European Construction Employers 2026"
- Automated: DB -> HTML -> PDF -> KDP upload

## Validated?
PARTIALLY. KDP data books sell but low-volume per title. Key: volume of titles not single bestseller. 50 titles x 5 copies/month x EUR 5 royalty = EUR 1,250/month.

## Price
EUR 15-19 (sector catalogs, 100-200 pages). EUR 29-49 (country directories, 300+ pages). Royalty 35-60% = EUR 5-20/sale. 200-page color 8.5x11" ~$12 print cost, sell at $39.99 = $24 profit.

## Risk
- LOW financial (zero upfront, print-on-demand)
- LOW-MEDIUM demand (business directories niche, low search volume)
- Freshness: data stales, need annual editions
- GDPR: use only public company data, no personal contacts

## Recommendation
GO as passive experiment. Publish 10 titles across sectors/languages. Cost: zero. Expected EUR 200-500/month after 3 months. Upside: niche audiences exist. Downside: low volume per title.
