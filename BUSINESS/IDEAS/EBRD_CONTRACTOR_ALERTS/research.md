# Research: EBRD CONTRACTOR ALERTS (IDEA-062)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Paid alert service for EBRD-financed procurement. We have 4,176 projects across 42 countries + 134 identified contractors. Subscribers get filtered tender alerts before competitors. EUR 49/month/subscriber.

## Market Size & Demand
- EBRD finances ~EUR 10-14B/year in projects across 38 member countries
- Thousands of contractors compete for EBRD-funded works, goods, consultancy
- Development bank procurement is a niche but high-value market (construction, infrastructure, consulting)
- TED publishes 700K+ procurement notices/year; EBRD is subset but high-value

## Our Advantage
- Already have 4,176 EBRD projects tracked + 134 contractors identified
- Procurement monitor scripts running (TED, SEAP, ECEPP, MTender)
- Can offer EBRD-specific filtering that generalist platforms lack
- EUR 49/mo significantly undercuts dgMarket ($83/mo) and GlobalTenders ($334/mo)
- Existing email infrastructure (2,500+/day) for alert delivery

## Market Validated?
YES partially. dgMarket, GlobalTenders, TendersOnTime all have paying subscribers for similar services. The niche (EBRD-specific) is underserved -- most platforms are generalist. Contractors actively seek early intelligence on MDB procurement.

## Price Point
EUR 49/mo is competitive. Market ranges EUR 83-399/mo for comparable services. Could tier: Basic EUR 29/mo (alerts only), Pro EUR 49/mo (alerts + contractor intel), Enterprise EUR 149/mo (custom filters + API).

## Risk
- MEDIUM. ECEPP is free (but no smart filtering). Competitors are established.
- Niche is small -- maybe 500-2000 potential subscribers globally.
- Need to maintain data freshness (already have cron jobs for this).

## Recommendation
**LAUNCH** -- Low effort (8h), infrastructure exists, clear price advantage. Start with email alerts to existing contractor database. Revenue potential EUR 3K-10K/mo with 60-200 subscribers.
