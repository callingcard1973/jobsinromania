# Research: CORPORATE RELOCATION PACKAGE (IDEA-131)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
End-to-end group worker relocation Romania to Norway. Documents, travel, housing, insurance, orientation. EUR 500/worker. 50 workers = EUR 25,000/deal.

## Market Size
- ~100,000 Romanians work in Norway (SSB data)
- ~5,000-10,000 new Romanian workers relocate to Norway annually
- Recruitment agency fee: EUR 650 (support) to EUR 950 (skilled) per worker
- Alternative: 20-30% of first annual salary as commission
- EURES relocation allowance: EUR 700-1,400/worker (EU-funded)
- Global relocation management: $18B (2025)

## Competitors
| Competitor | Type | Price | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|-------|
| NLS Norway Relocation Group | Full service | Custom | Largest in Norway, corporate HR focus |
| Relocation AS | Move management | Custom | Norway's largest relocation company |
| Pytheas.no | Immigration | Custom | HR/global mobility |

## Our Advantage
- buildjobs.eu with Norway pages LIVE, 15 agents in network
- EURES contacts established, Norwegian employer DB (TED construction)
- Existing campaign infrastructure reaching Norwegian employers
- Can claim EURES subsidies to offset costs

## Validated?
YES. Large market, competitors charge higher. EUR 500/worker is competitive. We already do recruitment + employer outreach. Gap: housing logistics and Norway on-ground support.

## Price
EUR 500/worker (20+). EUR 750 (5-19). EUR 1,000 (individual). Includes docs, travel, temp housing, insurance, orientation.

## Risk
- HIGH: Operational complexity (housing, transport, insurance). Need Norway partner. Liability for worker welfare. Cash flow upfront.

## Recommendation
GO with partnership model. Partner with NLS or Pytheas for Norway operations. We handle Romania-side. Start with one 20-worker pilot.
