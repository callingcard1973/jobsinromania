# Trade Agreement Arbitrage - Research Brief for Z.AI

## Context

Tudor runs InterJob - B2B recruitment/lead generation infrastructure with:
- 1.57M EU contract winners database (375K emails)
- 500K company records across 42 countries
- Email campaign system (10+ domains, 24/7)
- Enrichment pipeline (45+ scripts, 16 countries)
- Scraping capability for any public data

Tudor is Canadian citizen, based in Romania, with EU market access.

## Core Business Model

**Two-sided marketplace / broker:**
- Side A: Companies that NEED to import (have demand)
- Side B: Companies that CAN export (have supply)
- Tudor's role: Connect them, take commission (3-5%)

**Unique asset:** Database of EU buyers (TED winners = companies with procurement budgets)

   - Brazilian beef exporters (JBS, Minerva, Marfrig + smaller)

   - EU buyer database (Tudor has) + Mercosur supplier database (to acquire)

4. **Data sources to scrape/acquire:**

   - Brazilian Exporters Directory (10K+ companies)
   - Argentina Exporta

## RESEARCH TASK 4: CANADIAN TRADE AGREEMENTS

### Canada's FTA Network
- CETA (EU)
- USMCA (US, Mexico)
- CPTPP (Pacific nations)
- Canada-UK TCA
- Various bilateral FTAs

### What to explore:
1. **Triple arbitrage opportunities:**
   - Canada imports from CPTPP (Japan, Vietnam, etc.)
   - Re-exports to EU under CETA
   - Or vice versa

2. **Canada-specific advantages:**
   - Access to US market (USMCA)
   - Access to Pacific (CPTPP)
   - Access to EU (CETA)
   - Access to UK (TCA)

3. **Commodities with multi-FTA advantage:**
   - Which products can flow Canada → EU → elsewhere?
   - Which products can flow Asia → Canada → EU?

4. **Tudor's unique position:**
   - Canadian passport + Romanian residence
   - Can operate in both jurisdictions
   - Database of EU buyers
