# MERCOSUR - CETA - Romania Trade Brief (claude.md)

## Objective
Enable Tudor to execute a high-impact broker/lead-gen trade arbitrage strategy using existing EU buyer DB and new Mercosur + Canada supplier sourcing.

## 1) Ranked opportunities by fit
1. Mercosur -> EU food & battery metals
   - Beef, honey, soy/sugar, lithium, niobium
   - High volume + clear FTAs supporting reduced tariffs from May 2026
2. Canada -> EU specialty foods + minerals
   - Lobster, seafood, maple, lumber, equipment
   - CETA advantage + personal credibility (Canadian)
3. Romania/EU -> Mercosur input goods, IT services
   - Auto parts, machinery, wines, IT outsourcing
4. Triple arbitrage (CPTPP/USMCA -> CETA)
   - Niche: Japanese/Korean components via Canada into EU

## 2) Key data sources
- Mercosur exporters: APEX Brasil, Argentina Exporta, national exporter directories
- Trade shows: APAS/Fispal, Expoalimentaria exhibitor lists
- Commercial data: Volza, Tendata, ImportGenius, Panjiva, Tenders Electronic Daily
- Canadian sources: Trade Commissioner, EDC, Canadian CSR databases
- EU buyers: existing TED winners, country/sector filter via curated pipeline

## 3) Business structure recommendation
- Start with a Romanian/EU SRL as low-overhead hub for EU operations, handling outreach + matching.
- Register parallel Canadian entity for Canadian-origin deals and CETA favors, then route deals through both in commission split.
- Keep logistics and contract performance to partners (2PL) to stay asset-light.

- Scrape/acquire Mercosur exporter lists (APEX Brasil least friction). 
- Validate sample target buyers in EU TED data (100 top buyers per commodity).

Weeks 3-6

Weeks 7-12

- Onboard strategic sourcing partner in Mercosur and logistics partner in EU. 
- Generate first matched deal pitch; negotiate 3-5% commission template.

- Assumptions: 3 completed leads/month, avg deal size €500k, 4% commission → €60k/month, €720k/year.
- First 6 months ramp: monthly revenue run-rate from €0 to €40k. 
- Break-even target: 3 commercial wins within 90 days.
