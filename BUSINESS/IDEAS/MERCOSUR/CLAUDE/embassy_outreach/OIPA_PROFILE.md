# OIPA - Organization Profile

## Full Name
**Organizatia Interprofesionala a Producatorilor de Legume si Fructe**
(Interprofessional Organization of Vegetable and Fruit Producers)

## Website
- https://oipa.ro
- https://oipalegumefructe.ro

## Mission
A strong voice for agricultural producers of vegetables and fruits in Romania. Promoting sustainable development of the sector and facilitating access to national and international markets.
