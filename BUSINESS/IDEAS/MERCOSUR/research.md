# Research: MERCOSUR (IDEA-031)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
22 sectoral email campaigns targeting EU-Mercosur trade opportunities. Company registries from 4 countries (Argentina, Brazil, Uruguay, Paraguay) + embassy contacts. Exploit new trade agreement entering force 1 May 2026.

## Market Size
- EU-Mercosur creates trading zone of 700-780 million consumers, 30% of global GDP
- EU saves EUR 4B/year in tariffs; 91% of EU goods get tariff elimination
- 600,000+ EU jobs supported by Mercosur exports
- Key tariff reductions: cars (35% eliminated), machinery (14-20%), pharma (14%)
- Critical raw materials: EU imports 82% of Niobium from Mercosur
- Provisional application starts 1 May 2026 (all 4 Mercosur countries ratified)
- EU Parliament referred to CJEU (18-24 months for full ratification)

## Competitors
| Competitor | What | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|------|-------|----------|
| EU trade consulting firms | Big 4, boutique trade advisors | EUR 500+/hr | Expensive, not Romania-focused |
| Chambers of Commerce | Bilateral RO-BR, RO-AR chambers | Membership fees | Slow, networking-only, no data |
| AICEP (Portugal) | Trade promotion agency | Free | Portugal-focused, not RO |
| ProChile / ApexBrasil | Mercosur trade promotion | Free | Inbound only (they promote exports to EU) |
| Trade fair organizers | SIAL, Anuga for food trade | EUR 5K+/booth | Events, not data-driven campaigns |
| ASD Group | EU-Mercosur compliance consulting | Custom | Compliance focus, not lead gen |

## Our Advantage
- 7,600 emails/day capacity for mass outreach
- 500K+ company database (42 countries) for targeting EU companies
- Agreement enters force 1 May 2026 = perfect timing for "first-mover" consulting
- 22 pre-designed sectoral campaigns (beef, honey, lithium, machinery, pharma, etc.)
- Embassy/trade office contacts already collected
- Can position as Romania's bridge to Mercosur (low-cost EU operations base)

## Validated?
Partially. The trade agreement is real and entering force. EUR 4B/year in saved tariffs creates genuine demand. However: no specific client validation, no revenue from Mercosur campaigns yet. The 22 campaigns are designed but not tested.

## Price
Revenue model:
- Trade consulting: EUR 2,000-10,000 per company assisted
- Lead generation campaigns: EUR 500-2,000 per sector per country
- Commission on facilitated deals: 2-5%
- Data packages (Mercosur company registries): EUR 99-499

## Risk
- HIGH: Very early stage, no validated demand for Romania-Mercosur consulting
- Full ratification may take 18-24 months (CJEU review)
- Competition from established trade consulting firms
- Language barrier (Portuguese, Spanish) limits direct engagement
- 22 campaigns across 4 countries = too dispersed, need focus

## Recommendation
**CONDITIONAL GO - narrow focus** - Do NOT launch all 22 campaigns. Pick 2-3 sectors where Romania has natural advantage: (1) machinery/automotive (Romania is strong in auto parts), (2) agri-food (cooperative export to Brazil/Argentina), (3) critical raw materials (lithium for EU battery industry). Wait for provisional application (1 May 2026) then launch targeted campaigns. Revenue is speculative until first paying client.
