# Research: REVERSE JOBFAIR ONLINE (IDEA-099)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Norwegian employers post jobs on buildjobs.eu/no/, Romanian workers apply online. No physical fair. Already 5 landing pages LIVE. Scale unlimited.

## Market Size
- Norway construction sector: ~NOK 500B/year, chronic labor shortage
- FINN.no dominates Norway job boards (1.8M monthly users), custom pricing per listing
- Niche construction job boards exist globally but none for RO->NO corridor
- ~10,000 Romanians work in Norway; demand for more (welders, scaffolders, electricians)

- Can cross-sell to IDEA-096 (agencies) and IDEA-111 (CV database)
