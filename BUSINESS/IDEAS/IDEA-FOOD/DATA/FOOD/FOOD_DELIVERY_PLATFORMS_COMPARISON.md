## Detailed Pricing

### 1. GloriaFood (BEST FREE OPTION)
**Source:** [GloriaFood Pricing](https://www.gloriafood.com/pricing)

| Feature | Price |
|---------|-------|
| Online Ordering System | FREE forever |
| Table Reservations | FREE forever |
| Sales-Optimized Website | $9/mo |
| Promotional Marketing | $19/mo |
| Online Payments | $29/mo |
| Branded Mobile Apps | $59/mo |
| POS System | $49/mo |

**Verdict:** Best for single restaurants starting out

---

### 2. Deonde
**Source:** [Deonde Pricing](https://deonde.co/pricing.shtml)

| Plan | Price | Orders/mo | Extra Order |
|------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Starter | $49/mo | included | $0.04 |
| Pro | $99/mo | more | $0.04 |
| Premium | $199/mo | more | $0.03 |

**Additional:**
- Branded Mobile Apps: $499 one-time
- No setup fees
- Cancel anytime

**Verdict:** Good for startups, scales well

---

### 3. Shipday
**Source:** [Shipday Pricing](https://www.shipday.com/pricing)

| Plan | Price | Orders/mo |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Free | $0 | 300 |
| Professional | $39/mo | more |
| Elite | $99/mo | more |
| Business | $299/mo | unlimited |

**Features:**
- Unlimited drivers (all plans)
- No per-driver fees
- Integrations: Uber, Shopify, Toast, Lightspeed

**Verdict:** Best for delivery logistics only (not ordering)

---

### 4. Yelo (Jungleworks)
**Source:** [Yelo Pricing](https://jungleworks.com/yelo/pricing/)

| Feature | Price |
|---------|-------|
| Starting Price | $39/mo |
| Free Trial | 14 days |
| Mobile Apps | Add-on |
| Delivery Management | Add-on |
| Marketing Automation | Add-on |

**Features:**
- No-code platform
- 100+ payment gateways
- Geofencing
- Multi-vendor

**Verdict:** Good for hyperlocal businesses

### 5. Zeew
**Source:** [Zeew Pricing](https://zeew.eu/pricing-and-delivery-packages/)

| Orders/mo | Included | Extra Order |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| 100 | base | $0.10 |
| 1,000 | tier 2 | $0.10 |
| 2,000 | tier 3 | $0.10 |

**Additional:**
- Custom printer: $699 one-time
- Custom payment gateway: fixed fee
- EU-focused

**Verdict:** European market focus, good for EU expansion

---

### 6. Ordering.co
**Source:** [Ordering.co Pricing](https://www.ordering.co/pricing)

| Plan | One-Time | Stores | Orders/mo | Extra |
|------|----------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Startup Pro | $5,000 | 100 | 2,500 | $0.10 |
| Startup Platinum | $9,000 | 500 | 5,000 | $0.10 |
| Enterprise | $20,000 | 5,000 | 10,000 | $0.10 |

**Features:**
- White-label everything
- Unlimited support & updates
- Business & Driver apps
- API access (Platinum+)
- Open source code (Enterprise)

**Verdict:** Best for serious operators, one-time payment

### 7. SpotNeats
**Source:** [SpotNeats](https://www.spotneats.com/)

| Item | Price |
|------|-------|
| Clone Script | <$5,000 one-time |
| Custom Development | $20K-50K |
| Source Code | 100% ownership |

**Includes:**
- Customer app
- Driver app
- Restaurant dashboard
- Admin panel

**Verdict:** Full ownership, good for custom needs

---

### 8. Snap Delivered (Case Study)
**Source:** [Snap Delivered](https://ibos.snapdelivered.com/)

| Fee Type | Price |
|----------|-------|
| Per Delivery | $2 flat |
| Pro Plan (users) | $10/mo (free delivery) |
| Restaurant Commission | $0 (no %) |

**Philosophy:** "Delivery companies shouldn't make more off your food than you do"

**Verdict:** Revolutionary flat-fee model
