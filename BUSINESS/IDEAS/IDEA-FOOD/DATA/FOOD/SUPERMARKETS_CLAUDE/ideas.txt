   - Thousands of small producers (cheese, honey, sausages, jams) can't supply chains alone
   - Aggregate under one cooperative brand with unified HACCP/ISO
   - Approach Kaufland/Lidl/Carrefour as a single supplier

   - Legal: Legea 1/2005 privind cooperatia

6. COLD CHAIN GAP ANALYSIS
   - Only 159 cold storage facilities in the data — massive deficit
   - Map gaps: counties with producers but no cold storage
   - Invest or broker connections
   - Fundable via AFIR/PNDR Sub-measure 4.2

7. FARM-TO-FORK TRACEABILITY PLATFORM
   - EU Reg. 178/2002 requires traceability
   - Most small Romanian producers do it on paper
   - Build simple digital traceability tool (QR code per lot)
   - Entry ticket to selling to supermarket chains
   - Chains increasingly require digital traceability

8. SEASONAL SURPLUS MARKETPLACE
   - Massive seasonal surplus: tomatoes Aug-Sep, apples Sep-Oct, cabbage Oct-Nov
   - Connect surplus producers with processors, canners, juice makers in real-time
   - Both sides available in existing data
   - Reduce food waste, increase producer income
