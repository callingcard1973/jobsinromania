# Delivery Coverage Note

## Purpose
- This note defines the delivery position for the first wholesale outreach wave.
- It is intentionally conservative and excludes any promise that depends on unverified cold-chain operations.

## Wave 1 Delivery Position
- Focus on shelf-stable products only.
- Prioritize routes that can be served through standard courier, pallet, or consolidated wholesale delivery.
- Position Bucharest and major urban centers as the first commercial focus.

## Operational Assumptions
- Honey, preserves, juices, vinegar, and similar shelf-stable items can be consolidated and shipped without cold-chain.
- Delivery lead times, minimum order quantities, and pallet structure still need final confirmation from each included seller.
- Dairy and cheese are explicitly excluded from the first operational promise.

## Buyer-Safe Language
- Initial assortment is structured for shelf-stable delivery.
- The first rollout is intended for pilot wholesale and distribution conversations.
- Cold-chain expansion is possible later, after logistics readiness is validated.

## Still To Confirm
1. Dispatch origin for each included seller.
2. Consolidation point under the cooperative model.
3. Packaging constraints for pallet and mixed-case handling.
4. Lead times for replenishment.
5. Named logistics partner for larger wholesale deliveries.
