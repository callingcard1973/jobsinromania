## Our Advantage
- Already have the data: EBRD tenders, insolvency alerts (cifn.eu), job databases
- Existing automation (cron jobs, scrapers) can feed channels automatically
- Zero content creation cost -- data already flows through our systems

## Market Validated?
PARTIALLY. Telegram subscriptions work for trading/crypto/tech. Job alerts exist but are free (India-focused). EU tender alerts on Telegram = zero competition found. The question is: will EU professionals pay for Telegram channels when email newsletters exist?

## Price Point
- @JobAlerts EUR 5/mo -- realistic but hard to sell (free job alerts everywhere)
- @TenderAlerts EUR 19/mo -- most viable (professionals pay for procurement intel)
- @InsolvencyRO EUR 9/mo -- small niche but no competition
- Expected: 50-200 paying subscribers across all channels = EUR 500-2000/mo

## Risk
- MEDIUM. 60% subscriber churn after 3 months is brutal.
- Telegram audience skews younger/tech -- may not reach procurement professionals.
- Payment collection via Telegram bots can be clunky.
- Growing channels from zero requires marketing investment.
- InviteMember or similar bot needed for paywall ($29-99/mo cost).

## Recommendation
**BUILD** -- Start with @TenderAlerts (highest value, zero competition). Launch free channel first, build to 500+ subscribers, then gate premium content behind paywall. Use existing EBRD/TED data. Low effort (6h setup), test demand before investing more.
