## Our Advantage
- Direct access to workers through placement pipeline (100+ placements/mo)

## Market Validated?
PARTIALLY. Insurance brokers exist but none specialize in posted workers. The A1 certificate requirement creates mandatory demand. Workers must have coverage = guaranteed market.

## Price Point
- Commission: 10-20% of premium (industry standard)
- Average premium: EUR 50-200/mo per worker
- Commission per worker: EUR 5-40/mo
- 100 workers/mo x EUR 20 avg commission = EUR 2,000/mo
- Scale to 500 workers = EUR 10,000/mo

## Risk
- ASF (Financial Supervisory Authority) licensing required for brokerage
- Partnership negotiation with insurers takes time
- Low margin per worker unless high volume
- Regulatory complexity across EU countries
- Claims management overhead

## Recommendation
CONDITIONAL GO. High synergy with existing placement business. But requires ASF broker license or partnership with licensed broker. Best as add-on to IDEA-080/086, not standalone.
