# Research: PRODUS MONTAN (IDEA-016)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Catalog of 1,331 certified Romanian mountain product producers. WooCommerce marketplace + email campaign to connect producers with buyers. AGRIP EU grant deadline 23 April 2026.

## Market Size
- 1,507 registered mountain product producers in Romania (RNPM database)
- 4,686 certified mountain products across 8 categories
- EU mountain product certification (Reg. 1151/2012) provides legal protection and premium pricing
- AGRIP 2026 budget: EUR 205M for promotion of agricultural products (simple + multi programmes)
- Domestic retail channels: Kaufland "Din Romania", Lidl "Romaneste", Carrefour, Mega Image

## Competitors
| Competitor | What | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|------|-------|----------|
| produsmontan.ro | Official RNPM registry | Free | Raw data, no marketplace, no buyer matching |
| Tara Mea cooperative | 1,000+ member coop | N/A | Different product focus, not mountain-specific |
| Hypermarket own-label | Kaufland/Lidl sourcing programs | N/A | Buyers not competitors (potential clients) |
| Local farmer markets | Direct-to-consumer | N/A | No scale, no export |
| Diaspora food shops | Romanian products abroad | Retail | Fragmented, no aggregation |
| cumparlegume.com (own) | Vegetable buying platform | N/A | Different focus, complementary |

## Our Advantage
- Complete RNPM database already scraped and enriched (1,507 producers, 4,686 products)
- Catalog LIVE at agroevolution.com/catalog/ (9 sector pages + index + county filter)
- 666 producer emails with 30%+ proven response rate (Jan 2024 campaign)
- AGRIP grant application possible via OIPA (cooperative too young to apply alone)
- WooCommerce + WhatsApp CTA already integrated
- Cross-sell with Cooperativa (IDEA-011) and Trasabilitate (IDEA-012)

## Validated?
Yes. Jan 2024 campaign: 666 producers contacted, 201+ responded (30%+). Producers are eager to sell. Kaufland and Lidl actively source Romanian mountain products. AGRIP funding confirmed available.

## Price
Revenue model:
- Commission on marketplace transactions (5-10%)
- AGRIP grant co-financing (up to 80% for promotion)
- Annual catalog listing fee for producers (EUR 50-200)
- Buyer access subscription (EUR 99/month for distributors)

## Risk
- MEDIUM: AGRIP deadline is 23 Apr 2026 (9 days away) - tight
- Producer logistics/quality consistency
- WooCommerce marketplace needs cold chain/delivery integration
- Competition from hypermarket direct sourcing programs
- OIPA as lead applicant introduces dependency

## Recommendation
**GO - URGENT** - AGRIP deadline is 9 days away. Priority 1: submit AGRIP application. Priority 2: activate WooCommerce marketplace. The data asset and producer relationships are proven. Revenue comes from aggregation margins + grant funding. Pairs perfectly with Cooperativa (IDEA-011).
