### 9. Moldova cross-border trade

Romanian mountain products to Moldova (short distance, similar culture, no customs).
Import Moldovan fruits to Romania for cooperative members.

## AGRIP EU Promotion Grant 2026

**Program**: AGRIP -- Promotion of Agricultural Products (Reg. EU 1144/2014)
**Slogan**: "Enjoy, it's from Europe"
**2026 Budget**: EUR 205M total, EUR 160M as grants
**Deadline**: 23 April 2026, 17:00 CET (Brussels)
**15 topics** covering quality schemes, geographical indications, organic, sustainable

### Program Types
- **SIMPLE**: 1+ applicants from SAME EU country -> managed by APIA Romania
- **MULTI**: 2+ applicants from DIFFERENT EU countries -> managed by REA (Brussels)

### Who Can Apply
- Trade/inter-trade organizations representing 50%+ of sector producers
- Recognized producer organizations and associations (established 2+ years)
- NOT individual companies (must be sector-wide)
- **NOTE**: Gospodarii de Altadata is <2 years old -- CANNOT be lead applicant

### Eligible Quality Schemes
- PDO, PGI, TSG, EU organic logo, outermost region logos
- "Mountain product" (produs montan) = EU optional quality term under Reg. 1151/2012

### Application Strategy
- **Lead applicant**: OIPA (established trade org, on APIA mailing list)
- **WHY THIS WINS**: No Romanian campaigns exist + no mountain product campaigns exist = double novelty
- **Target markets**: Internal EU (hypermarkets + diaspora in IT/ES/DE/FR/UK) or UK post-Brexit
- **Activities**: Trade fairs (IFE London, Anuga Cologne, SIAL Paris), digital, tastings, B2B
- **Budget**: EUR 1-3M over 3 years (EU pays ~80%)
- **SLOGAN**: "Taste the Mountains -- Certified Romanian Mountain Products"
- **Alternative MULTI**: OIPA + French/Italian partner via UFE Roumanie

### How to Apply
1. Check eligibility: https://enjoy-its-from-europe.campaign.europa.eu
2. Submit via: https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/ (search AGRIP)
3. Guide: https://rea.ec.europa.eu/funding-and-grants/promotion-agricultural-products-0/how-apply-promotion-agricultural-products_en

### Info Day Recordings (Jan 27-28 2026, Brussels)

- Day 2: https://webcast.ec.europa.eu/info-day-on-the-calls-for-proposals-2026-for-promotion-of-agricultural-products-2026-01-28
