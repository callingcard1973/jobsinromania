# Research: JOB FAIR ORGANIZER (IDEA-092)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Virtual job fairs on .eu domains. Employers pay EUR 200-500/stand. 20 employers/fair = EUR 4,000-10,000/event. Already have 5,601 lines of code (DB+email+ANOFM+GDPR).

## Market Size
- Virtual career fair platform market: USD 1.28B (2025), projected USD 4.31B by 2033
- Broader online recruitment tech: USD 15.18B (2025), USD 46B by 2034
- Strong post-COVID adoption; hybrid events now standard

## Competitors
| Competitor | Pricing | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| vFairs | ~$14,925/event | Unlimited attendees, 3D booths, custom design |
| Brazen (Radancy) | Custom, $5K-20K/event | 1:1 video chat, scheduling, enterprise focus |
| Hopin | Custom, $5K+ | General events platform, breakout rooms |
| 6Connex | Custom, enterprise | 3D environments, Fortune 500 clients |
| Hubilo | Custom, expensive | AI matchmaking, video networking |
| Handshake | Free for students | University-focused, not open market |

## Our Advantage
- EUR 200-500/stand is 10-50x cheaper than vFairs/Brazen (they charge $5K-15K to the ORGANIZER, we charge per exhibitor)
- Own 15 .eu job domains with existing traffic and SEO
- 208M company database for employer outreach
- Email infrastructure (6,300/day) to invite employers
- ANOFM/EURES data for candidate sourcing
- Already built: DB, email, GDPR compliance

## Validated?
Partially. Virtual fairs are proven market. Our angle (cheap niche fairs on owned domains) is untested but low-risk given existing infra.

## Price
EUR 200-500/stand. Competitors charge organizers $5K-15K/event, then organizers charge $500-2,000/booth. We cut the middleman.

## Risk
Medium. Need critical mass of both employers and candidates per event. Quality perception at low price point. Technical: need video/chat features (build or integrate Jitsi).

## Recommendation
GO. Low cost to test. Run pilot fair on factoryjobs.eu or buildjobs.eu with 10 employers from existing DB. Use Jitsi for video. Revenue potential EUR 50K-120K/year with monthly fairs across domains.
