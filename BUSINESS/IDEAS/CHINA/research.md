# Research: CHINA TRADE (IDEA-026)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
3 scrapers (Made-in-China, NBS API, UN Comtrade) + 8 CSV trade indicators. Build trade intelligence for EU importers sourcing from China.

## Market Size
- Global trade data/intelligence services: ~$12.5B by 2030 (CAGR 8.7%)
- China = world's largest exporter (14.33% of global merchandise trade)
- Driven by supply chain complexity + data-driven procurement decisions

## Competitors

| Company | Type | Pricing | Notes |
|---------|------|---------|-------|
| Panjiva (S&P Global) | SaaS platform | ~$1,000+/mo (custom) | 130+ countries, bill of lading |
| ImportGenius | SaaS platform | ~$99-399/mo (public) | US customs focus |
| Tendata (EX DATA 6.0) | SaaS + China focus | Custom | Chinese leader, global acquisition |
| OEC (oec.world) | Free visualization | Free | Academic, no raw export |
| Trade Map (ITC/WTO) | Free/institutional | Free basic | Government-backed |
| WITS (World Bank) | Free bulk | Free | No alerts |

## Our Advantage
- Combined scraping (Made-in-China products + NBS macros + Comtrade flows) = unique cross-dataset
- 8 CSV indicators = pre-processed, ready for campaign targeting
- Can match Chinese suppliers to EU buyers from our 208M company DB
- Zero subscription cost (own scrapers vs $1K+/mo SaaS)
- Already have 933MB Chinese Industrial Enterprise Database (Figshare, 2.5M records)

## Validated?
Partially. Market is large but dominated by enterprise SaaS. Our angle = lead generation for EU importers, not competing as data platform.

## Price
- B2B lead lists: EUR 0.05-0.50/lead
- Monthly reports: EUR 200-500/subscriber
- Campaign service: EUR 50-100/targeted outreach batch

## Risk
- Made-in-China scraping may break (anti-bot)
- UN Comtrade has rate limits and delays
- Low differentiation unless combined with our employer DB

## Recommendation
PROCEED AS INTERNAL TOOL. Use trade data to enrich EU employer database with import/export activity. Sell as "EU importers buying X from China" lead lists via email campaigns. Do NOT build SaaS platform.
