# Research: FRESKON (IDEA-028)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Scrape and sell exhibitor contact data from European fresh produce trade fairs (FRESKON, Fruit Logistica, Fruit Attraction, etc.) for B2B lead generation.

## Market Size
- FRESKON 2025: 275 exhibitors from 22 countries, 2,000+ B2B meetings
- FRESKON 2026: 23-25 April 2026 in Thessaloniki
- Fruit Logistica (Berlin): 2,700+ exhibitors, 70,000+ trade visitors
- Fruit Attraction (Madrid): 2,000+ exhibitors, 100,000+ visitors
- Trade show lead retrieval market growing (LeadSuccess, Scan2Lead dominant in EU)
- Fresh produce trade is EUR 100B+ annually in EU

## Competitors
| Competitor | What | Price | Weakness |
|-----------|------|-------|----------|
| Helexpo (official) | Official exhibitor directory | Free on site | Only during event, no bulk export |
| Apify scrapers | Map Your Show exhibitor scraper | $5-50/run | Generic, needs customization |
| LeadSuccess/Scan2Lead | Trade fair lead capture tools | Per-event pricing | For exhibitors, not data resellers |
| Fruit Logistica catalog | Official exhibitor directory | Free PDF | No emails, limited contact data |
| ExpoDatabase | Trade fair exhibitor lists | $99-499 | Limited fresh produce focus |
| EventsEye | Fair directories/listings | Free browse | No contact data export |

## Our Advantage
- Already scraped 233 FRESKON 2025 exhibitors (17 columns, emails/phones included)
- Can scale to Fruit Logistica (2,700+), Fruit Attraction (2,000+), Asia Fruit Logistica
- Cross-match with PRODUS MONTAN producers and COOPERATIVA for buyer-supplier matching
- Automated scraping pipeline already built

## Validated?
Partially. Trade fair exhibitor data is valuable for sales teams. However, GDPR makes selling scraped personal contact data legally risky in Europe. The data is more valuable as an internal lead gen tool than a product to sell.

## Price
If sold:
- Single fair dataset: EUR 49-99
- Annual multi-fair bundle: EUR 199-399
- Custom scraping service: EUR 99-299 per fair

## Risk
- HIGH: GDPR compliance is the main blocker. Scraping exhibitor data and selling it without consent violates GDPR
- Trade fair organizers may have ToS prohibiting scraping
- Ryanair v. PR Aviation precedent: website operators can restrict scraping via ToS
- Legal risk outweighs revenue potential for external sales

## Recommendation
**NO-GO as a product to sell.** Use internally for: (1) buyer outreach for Cooperativa/Produs Montan exports, (2) equipment sourcing for sorting/packaging, (3) networking at FRESKON 2026. Do NOT sell scraped exhibitor data externally - GDPR risk too high for the revenue.
