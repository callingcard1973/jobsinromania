## Gerry Robert System (RESEARCHED 2026-04-15)

**It's a 40-HOUR writing system, not 40 steps.** Three systems: WRITE IT + PUBLISH IT + FUND IT.
His program costs $25K. We don't need it — we have the data and the pipeline.

### WRITE IT SYSTEM (40 hours)
1. Identify 15 core topics → select 10 as chapters
2. Generate 15-20 subtopics per chapter → narrow to 10-15
3. Create 3-5 questions per subtopic (~300-400 total)
4. Answer each question in 5-10 min increments
5. Write 30 min/day → manuscript in 2-3 months
**OUR ADVANTAGE:** We auto-generate from data. "Writing" = running a query. 40 hours → 4 hours.

### PUBLISH IT SYSTEM
6. Professional cover design ($250-500 via 99designs/Fiverr)
7. Editing and layout
8. ISBN (free from KDP or buy own)
9. Print-on-demand: Amazon KDP + IngramSpark (bookstores)
10. Formats: paperback + hardcover + Kindle
**OUR ADVANTAGE:** Playwright generates PDF. Cover via Pillow/Canva. Zero cost.

### FUND IT SYSTEM (genius part)
11. Sell ad space in book BEFORE writing it
12. Target companies serving your market (recruitment agencies, equipment sellers)
13. Charge EUR 500-2000/page for sponsor ads in back of book
14. Use sponsorships to fund production + launch
15. Host sponsored launch events
**OUR ADVANTAGE:** 18K agencies + 608 factory employers = sponsors who WANT visibility.

### MARKETING SYSTEM
16. "Book is 90% marketing, 10% content"
17. Give away free copies to build email database
18. Monetize through backend (API subscriptions, placements, consulting)
19. Collect endorsements pre-launch
20. Presale email list → free copies
21. Social media + LinkedIn campaigns
22. Book launch event (virtual or at job fair)
23. PR/media from "published author" positioning

### AUTHORITY FRAMEWORK
24. Book = business card that positions you as expert
25. Speaking invitations from book
26. Media/press coverage
27. Higher consulting rates
28. Partner credibility (EURES, AJOFM, embassies take you seriously)
