# Research: EU TENDER ALERTS NEWSLETTER (IDEA-068)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Paid newsletter: EU tenders filtered by sector (construction/food/IT). 5.1M tenders in TED database. EUR 19-49/mo. Delivery via Mailrelay (2,666/day free).

## Market Size & Demand
- EU public procurement = EUR 2 trillion/year (~14% of EU GDP)
- TED publishes 700K+ procurement notices/year
- Thousands of SMEs struggle to find relevant tenders in the noise
- Pain point is filtering -- TED is free but overwhelming

|-----------|-------|----------|-------|
| TED (free) | Free | Full database, basic search | No smart filtering, no email alerts |
| TENDERTRACK (tenders.eu) | <EUR 5/day (~EUR 150/mo) | Email alerts, predefined searches | Established, EU-focused |

## Our Advantage
- Already scraping TED (370K+ exports in DB)
- Mailrelay = 2,666 emails/day FREE (zero delivery cost)
- Can filter by NACE/CPV codes matching our sector expertise (construction, food, manufacturing)
- EUR 19-49/mo massively undercuts TENDERTRACK (EUR 150/mo) and GlobalTenders ($334/mo)
- Can combine with EBRD alerts (IDEA-062) for broader offering
- Existing email infrastructure handles delivery

## Market Validated?
YES. Multiple competitors with paying customers at EUR 83-399/mo. TENDERTRACK has been operating for years at <EUR 5/day. The market clearly pays for filtered tender alerts. TED is free but the filtering/alert layer is the value add.

## Price Point
EUR 19-49/mo is very competitive. Market ranges EUR 83-399/mo.
- Basic EUR 19/mo: 1 sector, daily email digest
- Pro EUR 49/mo: 3 sectors, instant alerts, AI summaries
- Enterprise EUR 149/mo: unlimited sectors, API access, custom filters
- Free tier: weekly digest (funnel to paid)

## Risk
- MEDIUM. Competitors are established but expensive.
- Tendify (AI-powered, well-funded) could commoditize the market.
- Tenderio is EU-funded and free -- could kill paid newsletters.
- Need consistent data freshness (already have scrapers).
- Content quality (filtering accuracy) is the key differentiator.

## Recommendation
**LAUNCH** -- Excellent fit. We have the data (370K TED exports), delivery (Mailrelay free), and can price 3-5x below competitors. Start with construction sector (our strongest data). Launch free weekly digest, convert to paid daily alerts. Combine with IDEA-062 (EBRD alerts) for a comprehensive procurement intelligence product.
