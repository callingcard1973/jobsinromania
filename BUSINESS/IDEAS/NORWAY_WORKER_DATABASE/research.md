# Research: NORWAY WORKER DATABASE (IDEA-111)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Collect CVs from Romanians wanting Norway jobs via buildjobs.eu/no/. Sell access to Norwegian agencies. EUR 20-50/CV. Become the supply side.

## Market Size
- Recruitment database access: $15-150/user/month (small tools) to $500-2,000/month (enterprise)
- CV database access: eFinancialCareers charges per-search or subscription for 1M+ candidate profiles
- Norway staffing market: EUR 2.7B/year, agencies pay 15-25% of annual salary per placement
- One placement (welder in Norway, EUR 40K salary) = EUR 6K-10K agency fee

## Competitors
| Competitor | Price | Model |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| LinkedIn Recruiter | $170-270/mo/seat | 1B+ profiles, search + InMail |
| eFinancialCareers CV Search | Custom | Finance-specific, 1M+ CVs |
| Recruitee | $199-299/mo | ATS + sourcing |
| Bullhorn | $99-199/mo | Staffing-focused ATS |
| EURES CV Online | Free | EU-wide, limited quality |
| eJobs/BestJobs (Romania) | EUR 200-500/mo | Romania-focused |

## Our Advantage
- Only database specifically for "Romanians wanting Norway construction jobs"
- Hyper-niche = high value per CV (agencies pay EUR 6K-10K per placement)
- buildjobs.eu/no/ already LIVE (5 pages)
- Can collect CVs organically (SEO) + via newsletter (IDEA-104) + via campaigns
- GDPR-compliant if workers consent explicitly

## Validated?
Partially. CV databases are proven business. The RO->NO niche is untested but agencies clearly need workers.

## Price
- Per CV: EUR 20-50 (verified, with skills/experience)
- Subscription: EUR 199-499/mo for unlimited access
- Success fee: EUR 500-1,000 per placement (higher margin, harder to track)

## Risk
Medium. Need critical mass of CVs before agencies will pay (chicken-and-egg). GDPR compliance essential. CV quality varies; need verification. Agencies may recruit directly after first purchase.

## Recommendation
GO but phase it. Phase 1: add CV upload form to buildjobs.eu/no/ (free). Phase 2: collect 200+ CVs. Phase 3: contact Norwegian NACE 78.xx agencies offering free trial access. Phase 4: convert to paid. Synergy with IDEA-096, IDEA-099, IDEA-104.
