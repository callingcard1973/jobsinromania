# Research: AGENTII (IDEA-004)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Email campaign to 18K EU recruitment agencies proposing worker placement partnerships. Revenue from placement fees (EUR 3,000-5,000 per worker placed through partner agencies).

## Market Size & Demand
- EURES network: 1,000+ advisers across 31 countries, 270+ member/partner organizations
- EURES portal: 3 million job vacancies, 1 million CVs
- EU recruitment agency fees: 15-30% of first-year salary (median), translating to EUR 3,000-10,000 per blue-collar placement
- Romania 2026 labor quota: 100,000 work visas available for foreign workers
- Bulgaria labor deficit: 262,000 workers needed (2025), construction alone ~23,000
- We already have 18K agency contacts in PostgreSQL — verified, enriched database
- If 1% convert to active partners = 180 agencies = potential for 500-1,000 placements/year

## Our Advantage
- **18K contacts already in database** — no acquisition cost, ready to campaign
- **Email infrastructure**: 6,300 emails/day capacity, multi-provider (Brevo, Gmail, Zoho, Mailrelay)
- **Existing placement network**: Already running campaigns for construction, factory, HORECA, care workers
- **Multi-language capability**: 11 languages for outreach across EU
- **Domain portfolio**: 15 job domains (careworkers.eu, factoryjobs.eu, buildjobs.eu, etc.) adds credibility
- **Low effort**: 3 hours to launch campaign — templates exist, infrastructure ready

## Market Validated? YES
- Recruitment is a EUR 200B+ global industry; EU is ~30% of that
- Placement fees of EUR 3,000-5,000 per worker are standard and well-documented
- Labor shortages across EU (construction, HORECA, healthcare) create constant demand
- Existing campaigns (Harghita, ANOFM, EBRD) prove email outreach works for recruitment
- Agency Leads charges $486-625/mo just for the DATABASE — we already have the data

## Price Point
- No subscription fee — this is a partnership/commission model
- Revenue per placement: EUR 3,000-5,000 (split with partner agency)
- Volume target: 50 placements/year = EUR 150K-250K revenue
- Estimated annual revenue at scale: EUR 420,000 (per original estimate)

## Risk
- **Low conversion rate** — cold email to agencies may yield <1% response
- **Trust barrier** — agencies need to verify our legitimacy before partnering
- **Payment collection** — cross-border invoicing for placement fees
- **Competition from direct hiring** — employers increasingly bypass agencies
- **Data freshness** — 18K contacts need verification (emails may bounce)
- **Regulatory** — some EU countries require licensing for recruitment intermediation

## Recommendation: LAUNCH
Zero additional data cost (18K contacts ready), minimal setup (3h), massive revenue potential. Campaign is essentially free to run. Even 0.5% conversion = 90 partner agencies. Risk is only time spent on email sending.
