## Description

Romanian public procurement is worth billions annually, but most companies lose bids not because they lack capability — they lose because their proposals are priced wrong, structured poorly, or miss the scoring criteria.

The SEAP Bidding Assistant analyzes your CPV codes against 6.2 million TED award records (ted.europa.eu) and Romania's own e-licitatie.ro database to tell you what winning bids actually look like: who wins, at what price, and how often. It then generates a complete bid proposal in Romanian — technically accurate, formatted to SEAP specifications, and priced within the competitive range for that specific tender category.

You supply the tender notice URL, your company profile, and your target margin. The system delivers a ready-to-submit proposal document: executive summary, technical approach, methodology, pricing justification, and compliance checklist.

Used by construction firms (CPV 45xxx), IT suppliers (CPV 48xxx/72xxx), and services companies (CPV 73xxx/79xxx) across Romania. Works on both direct acquisitions (achizitii directe) and open procedure tenders (procedura deschisa).

The 5% success fee is payable only on awarded contracts — if you do not win, you pay nothing beyond the base fee.

- **Historical winner analysis** — top 10 contractors by CPV code, win rates, and price distribution (min/median/max) from 6.2M TED awards
- **Competitive price benchmarking** — know where to price before you submit; avoid the two failure modes (too high = disqualified, too low = margin destruction)

- **Compliance checklist** — eligibility criteria mapped to your company documents so nothing is missing at submission

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What CPV codes do you cover?**
A: All CPV divisions with sufficient historical data — primarily construction (45xxx), IT and software (48xxx, 72xxx), consulting and services (73xxx, 79xxx), and facilities management (90xxx). Niche CPVs with fewer than 50 historical awards may have limited benchmarking data; we will tell you upfront if that is the case.

**Q: How is the 5% success fee calculated and collected?**
A: The fee applies to the awarded contract value, not the bid value. You notify us when the contract is signed and pay within 30 days. We track awarded contracts through the SEAP public register, but self-reporting speeds up the process and avoids reconciliation disputes.

**Q: Can you help with tenders already in progress, or only new ones?**
A: Both. For tenders with deadlines under 5 business days we charge a 50% rush surcharge on the base fee. For tenders already submitted but not yet awarded, we can prepare a clarification response or a revised offer if the procedure allows it.
