- Complete system: 28 domains + 208M DB + email campaigns + LLM pipeline

YES. Express Employment has 900+ franchise locations. White label ATS market thriving. But nobody offers our full-stack bundle (data + domains + campaigns + LLM). Unique positioning.

## Price Point
- Starter: EUR 500/mo (1 country, 5 domains, basic DB access)
- Professional: EUR 1,000/mo (3 countries, 15 domains, full campaigns)
- Enterprise: EUR 2,000/mo (unlimited countries, all 28 domains, LLM pipeline)
- Setup fee: EUR 1,000-3,000 one-time
- vs traditional franchise $25K-100K upfront

## Risk
- Highest effort of all ideas (40h initial + ongoing support)
- Need to productize/document the entire system
- Support overhead per licensee
- Data sharing across borders = GDPR complexity
- Licensee quality control: bad actors can damage brand
- Revenue depends on finding agencies willing to pay monthly

## Recommendation
CONDITIONAL GO. Highest revenue potential (EUR 5K-20K/mo at scale) but highest effort. Best executed AFTER proving 2-3 simpler ideas (073, 076, 082). The system needs documentation and a self-service portal before franchising.
