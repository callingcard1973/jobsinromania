===============================================

Q: What is TED?
A: Tenders Electronic Daily - the official EU public procurement database. All public contracts above threshold values are published here.

Q: How fresh is the data?
A: Covers 2020-2025 contract awards.

Q: What format?
A: CSV file, works with Excel, Google Sheets, any CRM.

Q: Are emails verified?
A: Yes, all records have business email addresses.

Q: Can I use for cold email?
A: Yes, for B2B purposes. This is public business data.
