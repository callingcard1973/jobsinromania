## TIER 1: Digital Product Marketplaces (Easiest)

### 1. Payhip
**URL:** https://payhip.com
**Fee:** 5% + payment fees
**Payout:** PayPal, Stripe
**Best for:** All digital products, lowest fees

### 2. Gumroad
**URL:** https://gumroad.com
**Fee:** 10% + $0.50 + payment fees
**Payout:** Bank transfer (no PayPal)
**Best for:** Brand credibility, discovery

### 3. Lemonsqueezy
**URL:** https://lemonsqueezy.com
**Fee:** 5% + $0.50
**Payout:** Bank transfer
**Best for:** Subscriptions, EU tax compliance

### 4. Sellfy
**URL:** https://sellfy.com
**Fee:** $19-99/month, 0% transaction
**Payout:** PayPal, Stripe
**Best for:** High volume sellers

### 5. Ko-fi
**URL:** https://ko-fi.com
**Fee:** 0% (free) or 5% (Gold)
**Payout:** PayPal, Stripe
**Best for:** Small products, tips
