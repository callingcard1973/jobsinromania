## Purpose

Feasibility analysis for **Gospodarii de Altadata Cooperativa Agricola (CAP)** as a federation of agricultural cooperatives to supply bulk products to institutional procurement channels (Romanian SEAP/SICAP, NATO, UN).

## Scope

- Romania-based agricultural cooperative federation
- Bulk agricultural and food products supply
- Institutional procurement: SEAP/SICAP, NATO contractors, UNGM
- Technical, analytical feasibility study
