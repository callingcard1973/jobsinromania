# Quick Start: CAP Federation Implementation
**"From Zero to First Contract in 12 Weeks"**

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**What:** Establish CAP Federation (15 cooperatives, bulk food supplier)
**Target Revenue:** 50K-100K EUR (Month 4)
**Total Investment:** 75K-100K EUR (Phase 1, Months 1-4)
**Timeline:** 12 weeks to first revenue
**Feasibility:** HIGH (proven by NISARA/MATRA existing model)

## WEEK 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1-7)

### Do This Week:

1. **Kickoff Meeting (Day 1)**
   - Gather founding team (3-5 people)
   - Review feasibility study: `reports/executive_feasibility_study.md`
   - Approve 75K-100K EUR budget
   - Assign roles (see below)

2. **Assign Role Owners:**
   ```
   Board Chair (Strategic): Responsible for governance
   Legal Counsel (Legal): Contracts, ORC registration
   Business Dev (Sales): Co-op recruitment, supplier partnerships
   Executive Director (Operations): Hiring, daily tasks (hire by Week 4)
   Finance (Treasury): Funding, banking, accounting
   ```

3. **Draft Key Documents (Days 2-7):**
   ```
   Priority: Constitution > Membership Agreement > Bylaws
   Time: 2-3 days for initial drafts
   Review: Week 2 with legal counsel
   ```

4. **Create Prospect Database (Days 3-7):**
   ```bash
   Command Line Tool:
   cd /opt/ACTIVE/IDEAS/NATO/OPENCODE
   python3 scripts/create_coop_database.py
   
   Output:
   data/prospective_coops.csv (target: 50+ profiles)
   ```

5. **Research Military Suppliers (Days 4-7):**
   - Focus: NISARA IMPEX, MATRA, EUROGRUP BOGDAN
   - Extract contacts from SEAP data
   - Prepare outreach email template

## FAILURE MODES & RECOVERY

### If Week 1-3 Fails (No ORC, No Co-ops):
- **Recovery:** Extend Phase 1 by 4-6 weeks
- **Action:** Expand prospect list to 100 co-ops, use interim partnership (legal handshake)
- **Impact:** First revenue delayed to Month 6-7 (vs Month 4)

### If Week 4-5 Fails (No Subcontract):
- **Recovery:** Bid directly on SEAP small contracts (25K-50K EUR)
- **Action:** Focus on county councils (lower competition)
- **Impact:** Lower initial revenue (25K-50K vs 50K-100K)

### If Week 6-7 Fails (No First Delivery):
- **Recovery:** Revisit subcontractor agreements (quality issues?)
- **Action:** Quality control audit, adjust procedures
- **Impact:** Revenue delayed 2-4 weeks
