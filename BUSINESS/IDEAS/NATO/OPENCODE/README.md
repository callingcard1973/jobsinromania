# Project: Gospodarii de Altadata Cooperativa Agricola (CAP) - Feasibility Study

**Status:** Analysis Complete | **Decision:** HIGH FEASIBILITY | **Recommendation:** PROCEED

## Project Overview

Feasibility analysis for structuring **Gospodarii de Altadata Cooperativa Agricola (CAP)** as a federation of agricultural cooperatives to supply bulk agricultural and food products to institutional procurement channels:
- SEAP/SICAP (Romania public procurement)
- NATO procurement (Romania bases + NSPA)
- UN procurement (UNGM, primarily WFP)

## Key Findings Summary

### Market Opportunity

| Channel | Annual Value | Federation Target (Year 3) | Timeline to First Revenue |
|---------|-------------|----------------------------|---------------------------|
| SEAP/SICAP | 1.4B EUR | 1-2M EUR | 6-9 months |
| Military (Romania) | 200M EUR | 0.5-1M EUR | 4-6 months (subcontracting) |
| NSPA (NATO) | 500M+ EUR | 0.3-0.8M EUR | 11-14 months |
| UNGM (UN) | 50-100M EUR | 0.2-0.5M EUR | 11-14 months |

**Combined 5-Year Revenue Projection: 15M-35M EUR**

### Competitive Advantage

- Price advantage: 5-12% lower costs vs existing distributors
- Geographic reach: Multi-county coverage (10+ counties)
- Seasonal resilience: Multi-zone production
- Quality standardization: Unified ISO 22000/HACCP

### Investment Required

- Initial setup (legal, certifications): 100K-150K EUR
- Operating reserve (18-24 months): 200K-250K EUR
- **Total: 300K-400K EUR**

### Feasibility Rating: **HIGH**

- Timeline to first revenue: 4 months (subcontracting)
- Break-even: 11-12 months (conservative), 6-8 months (aggressive)
- 5-Year ROI: 37× (conservative) to 74× (aggressive)

## Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-4)
- Action: Legal setup, recruit 15 cooperatives, SEAP registration
- Milestone: First subcontract (Month 4): 50K-100K EUR

### Phase 2: Scale-Up (Months 5-9)
- Action: Direct SEAP bidding, ISO 9001/22000 certification, expand to 20-25 co-ops
- Milestone: First direct award (Month 6-7): 75K-150K EUR

### Phase 3: NATO/UN Entry (Months 10-14)
- Action: NSPA/Ungm registration, first NATO/UN tenders
- Milestone: First NSPA/UN award (Month 11-14): 100K-250K EUR

### Phase 4: Consolidation (Months 15-24)
- Action: Framework agreements, 35+ cooperatives, 2-4.5M EUR annual revenue

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Multi-county membership** (15-35 cooperatives, 5-12 counties)
2. **Unified quality certification** (HACCP, ISO 9001, ISO 22000)
3. **Subcontracting pathway** (build track record with NISARA/MATRA)
4. **Working capital reserve** (100K-200K EUR minimum)
