## SEAP/SICAP Contract Types

### 1. Achizitie Directa (Direct Acquisition)
- Fast track procurement
- Value threshold: up to 115,000 RON (~23,000 EUR)
- Simple procedure, no formal tender
- Suitable for: Small bulk orders (1-5 tons)

### 2. Achizitie Simplificata (Simplified Procurement)
- Value: 115,000 - 1,000,000 RON (23,000 - 200,000 EUR)
- Can be used for: Regular bulk orders (5-20 tons)
- Publication: SEAP platform, 15-day minimum bid period

### 3. Licitatie (Full Tender)
- Value: >1,000,000 RON (>200,000 EUR)
- Formal procedure with multiple rounds
- Suitable for: Large contracts (20+ tons, annual supply)
- Publication: SEAP + TED (Europe-wide)
