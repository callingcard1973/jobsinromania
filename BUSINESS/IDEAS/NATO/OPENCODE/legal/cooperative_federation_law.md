# Legal Framework: Cooperative Federation in Romania

## Relevant Legislation

### 1. Law 1/2005 - Legea Cooperativiei (Cooperative Law)

**Key Provisions for Federations:**

**Article 73: Cooperative Federation**
- Cooperatives can form federations for:
  * Mutual assistance
  * Common economic objectives
  * Market access
  * Negotiation with institutions

**Article 74: Federation Membership**
- Minimum 5 cooperatives
- Each member cooperative retains autonomy
- Federation has legal personality
- Governance: representative assembly from members

**Article 75: Federation Purpose**
- Economic activity (commercial)
- Representation of member interests
- Facilitation of trade
- Risk management

### 2. Law 36/1991 - Agricultural Companies Law

**Applicable if CAP structured as agricultural company:**
- Minimum 5 members (cooperatives or individuals)
- Capital: minimum RON equivalent of 20,000 EUR
- Registration: ORC (Registrul Comerțului)
- Tax: 16% corporate income tax

### 3. ORC Registration Requirements

**To register CAP as legal entity:**

1. **Constitutional Document:**
   - Founding agreement (contract de asociere)
   - Articles of association
   - Federation bylaws

2. **Required Information:**
   - Name (unique, not misleading)
   - Address (Romania)
   - Object of activity (CAEN codes)
   - Contributing cooperatives
   - Initial capital structure

3. **CAEN Codes (Activity Classifications):**
   - Primary: 0161 - Activities supporting crop production
   - Secondary: 4621 - Wholesale of agricultural raw materials
   - Secondary: 4661 - Wholesale of fruit and vegetables

4. **Processing Time:**
   - Standard: 3-5 working days
   - Expedited: 1 day (additional fee)
   - Cost: ~500-800 RON

### 4. Taxation

#### Federation (CAP) Level
| Tax Type | Rate | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| Corporate Income Tax | 16% | On net profit |
| VAT | 19% | If revenue > 300,000 RON/year, mandatory |
| Micro-enterprise tax | 1-3% | If revenue < 1M EUR (optional) |

#### Member Cooperative Level
| Tax Type | Rate | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| Agricultural income tax | 10% | On income from primary production |
| Agricultural land tax | Exempt | Agricultural land is tax-exempt |
| VAT | 19% | On sales to federation |

### 5. Contracting Framework

**Model: CAP as Aggregator/Prime Contractor**

```
Member Cooperative → CAP → Institutional Buyer
     (Supplier)          (Prime Contractor)
          ↓                    ↓
    Price A              Price B
                          (Margin: 5-15%)
```

**Required Contracts:**

**1. Membership Agreement (Cooperative ↔ CAP)**
- Duration: Minimum 1 year
- Commitments:
  * Minimum volume commitments
  * Quality standards (HACCP specifications)
  * Delivery schedule
  * Price mechanism (fixed, indexed, or spot)
  * Payment terms (CAP pays within 30 days)
- Legal basis: Law 1/2005, Art. 77

**2. Supply Contract (CAP ↔ Institutional Buyer)**
- CAP is prime contractor
- CAP bears full responsibility for delivery
- CAP coordinates with member cooperatives
- Legal enforceability against CAP entity

**3. Non-Compete/Exclusivity (Optional)**
- Members can sell elsewhere
- CAP has first right of refusal on surplus
- Reasonable to prevent arbitrage

### 6. Liability Mechanisms

**CAP Liability to Buyers:**
- Full contractual liability
- Performance guarantees (if required)
- Centralized quality assurance

**Member Liability to CAP:**
- Breach penalties in membership agreement
- Performance bonds (5-10% of contract value)
- Dispute resolution: Arbitration clause

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Diversified membership (minimum 10 cooperatives)
- Geographic distribution (multiple counties)
- Product diversity (multiple categories)
- Quality control checkpoints

### 7. Governance Structure

**Recommended Model (for operational efficiency):**

```
General Assembly (All Member Co-ops)
           ↓
Board of Directors (7-11 members, elected)
           ↓
Executive Director (Paid, full-time)
           ↓
Operational Teams:
  - Commercial
  - Logistics
  - Quality/Compliance
  - Finance
```

**Decision-Making:**
- General Assembly: Major decisions (new membership, bylaws changes)
- Board: Strategic decisions (contract bidding, partnerships)
- Executive Director: Operational decisions (delivery, coordination)

**Voting Rights:**
- One vote per member cooperative
- OR weighted by production volume (alternative model)

### 8. SEAP/SICAP Eligibility

**Federation Eligibility:**
- Yes - legal entities can bid
- CAP must be registered with ORC
- Must have HACCP/ISO certification
- Must have financial stability (no debt to state)

**Advantages as Federation:**
- Can bid on larger contracts (>100,000 RON)
- Geographic coverage (multiple counties)
- Volume capacity (multiplied by members)

**Registration with SEAP:**
- Account: https://sicap.e-licitatie.ro
- Documents required:
  * ORC certificate
  * Fiscal certificate
  * Tax clearance certificate
  * CUI
  * Proof of activity (financial statements)

### 9. NSPA (NATO) Eligibility

**Requirements:**
- Legal entity in NATO country ✓ (Romania)
- 3 years audited financial statements (or equivalent)
- ISO 9001 certification (quality management)
- ISO 22000 or HACCP (food safety)
- Supply chain traceability

**Federation-Specific Consideration:**
- CAP must demonstrate coordinated supply chain
- QC system across multiple production sites
- Centralized documentation and audit access

### 10. UNGM (UN) Eligibility

**Requirements:**
- Legal entity status ✓
- Financial stability (2 years statements)
- Product catalog
- Food safety certifications
- References (preference for existing contracts)

**Federation Advantage:**
- Volume commitment capability
- Diversified supply reduces risk
- Single point of contact for UN

### 11. Capital Structure

**Minimum Recommended Capital:**
- Initial: 50,000 EUR (to demonstrate solvency)
- Sources:
  * Membership fees (from cooperatives)
  * Equity contributions
  * Operational reserves (future profits)

**Funding Model Options:**

**Option A: Membership Fee Only**
- One-time joining fee: 10,000 RON
- Annual membership fee: 1,000 RON
- CAP takes margin: 5-15% on contracts

**Option B: Commission Model**
- No joining fee
- Commission on all sales through CAP: 10%
- Members get 90% of contract value

**Option C: Hybrid (Recommended)**
- Small joining fee: 5,000 RON
- Commission: 8% on sales
- Performance bonus for >95% delivery reliability

### 12. Intellectual Property

**Brand Ownership:**
- CAP owns brand "Gospodarii de Altadata CAP"
- Members can use brand on products sold through CAP
- Marketing materials created jointly

**Product Specifications:**
- CAP owns quality specifications
- Members must meet specifications
- CAP reserves right to reject non-compliant products

### 13. Dispute Resolution

**Internal (CAP ↔ Members):**
- Mandatory mediation first
- Arbitration clause: Cluj-Napoca Arbitration Court
- Governing law: Romanian law

**External (CAP ↔ Buyers):**
- Arbitration clause (depending on contract)
- SEAP: Administrative Chamber of Appeals
- NATO/UN: Specific dispute mechanisms (to be researched)

### 14. Data Protection

**GDPR Compliance:**
- Buyer data: processed by CAP
- Member data: limited access
- Performance data: shared for improvement

**Commercial Confidentiality:**
- Contract prices: protected
- Supplier identities: can be protected in contracts
- Business strategy: confidential
