## Risk Assessment

### Military Procurement Risks

1. **High competition from established suppliers**
   - Mitigation: Subcontracting first, then direct bidding
   - Timeline: Build track record before major bids

2. **Strict quality/packaging requirements**
   - Mitigation: NSPA-approved supplier guidance, military specifications
   - Timeline: Invest in quality systems early (ISO 22000)

3. **Payment delays (government contracts)**
   - Mitigation: Working capital reserve (6 months of operations)
   - Financial: 100K-200K EUR reserve

4. **NATO requirements beyond Romanian SEAP**
   - Mitigation: Early NSPA registration, NATO-specific certifications
   - Timeline: 6-9 months ahead of first NATO tender

5. **Seasonal supply variance (cooperative production)**
   - Mitigation: Multi-county membership, storage capacity
   - Operational: Geographic diversification (10+ counties)

### Mitigations Summary
