| Procurement Channel | Annual Volume | Federation Target (Year 3) | Target Share |

| NSPA (NATO) | 500M+ EUR (global) | 0.3-0.8M EUR | 0.06-0.15% |
| UN WFP | 50-100M EUR (region) | 0.2-0.5M EUR | 0.2-0.5% |

**Total Addressable Market: ~1.7-2B EUR (Romania + NATO + UN region)**
**Federation Year 3 Target: 2-4.5M EUR (0.1-0.3% of TAM)**

### Competitive Landscape

Existing distributors dominate but have fragmentation:
- MATRA: 84M EUR (1,342 contracts) - 6% of SEAP food market
- NISARA IMPEX: 40M EUR (669 contracts) - 3% of SEAP food market
- SIMULTAN: 53M EUR (206 contracts) - higher contract values

**Federation Opportunity:**
- Price advantage: 5-12% lower margin structure
- Geographic reach: Multi-county vs single-county distributors
- Resilience: Seasonal diversification vs single-region concentration
- Scale: 200M EUR total military procurement = major opportunity

## Commercial Model

### Revenue Structure

1. **Aggregation Margin: 8-13%**
   - Coordination: 2-3%
   - Logistics: 5-8%
   - Quality control: 1-2%
   - Compared to existing distributors: 15-25%

2. **Membership Fee (Optional):**
   - One-time: 5K EUR for founding cooperatives
   - Alternative: Commission-only model (no joining fee)

3. **Payment Flow:**
   - Customer → CAP (prime contractor)
   - CAP → Member cooperatives (90-92% of contract value)
   - CAP retains 8-13% margin

### Initial Membership Target

| Phase | Cooperatives | Counties | Production Capacity | Target Products |
|-------|--------------|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Foundation | 15 | 3-4 | 5,000 tons/year | Wheat, rice, potatoes, dairy, vegetables |
| Scale | 20-25 | 8-10 | 8,000 tons/year | Full spectrum (add meat, fruit) |
| Expansion | 35+ | 12+ | 12,000+ tons/year | All categories + processed foods |

## Success Factors

### Critical (Must-Have)

1. **Multi-county Cooperative Membership**
   - Minimum: 15 cooperatives, 3-4 counties, 5,000 tons/year
   - Target Year 3: 35+ cooperatives, 12+ counties, 12,000+ tons/year

2. **Unified Quality Certification**
   - HACCP: 2-3 months (mandatory baseline)
   - ISO 9001: 6-12 months (SEAP advantage)
   - ISO 22000: 9-12 months (NSPA/UN mandatory)

3. **Strategic Subcontracting Pathway**
   - Partner with existing military suppliers (NISARA, MATRA)
   - Build track record before major direct bids
   - Reduce initial market entry risk

4. **Working Capital Reserve**
   - Minimum: 100K EUR (6 months operations)
   - Recommended: 200K EUR (12 months operations)

### Important (Enablers)

5. **Experienced Executive Leadership**
   - Full-time Executive Director
   - Supply chain expertise
   - Military/NATO procurement experience

6. **Legal & Compliance Expertise**
   - Cooperative federation lawyer
   - Food safety/regulatory specialist
   - NSPA/UN registration assistance

7. **Logistics Network**
   - Multi-county collection system
   - Centralized aggregation hubs
   - External distribution partnerships

8. **Technology Infrastructure**
   - Supply chain tracking system
   - Quality control database
   - Traceability software

## Final Feasibility Verdict

**Overall Feasibility: HIGH**

**Key Drivers:**
1. Large market: 1.4B EUR (Romanian SEAP food) + 200M EUR (military)
2. Proven model: Existing distributors (MATRA, NISARA) demonstrate market size
3. Competitive advantage: 5-12% price advantage via federation structure
4. Multiple entry paths: Subcontracting → Direct SEAP → NSPA → UN
5. Low regulatory barriers: Romania cooperative law fully enables federation model

**Timeline to Revenue:**
- First revenue (subcontracting): 4 months
- First direct award: 6-7 months
- First NSPA/UN award: 11-14 months
- Break-even: 18-24 months cumulative

**Investment Required:**
- Initial setup (legal, certifications): 100K-150K EUR
- Operating reserve (18-24 months): 200K-250K EUR
- **Total: 300K-400K EUR**

**Projected ROI (5 Years):**
- Conservative: 14.85M EUR revenue vs 400K EUR investment (37× return)
- Aggressive: 29.7M EUR revenue vs 400K EUR investment (74× return)
- Break-even month: 11-12 (conservative) or 6-8 (aggressive)

**Recommended Go Decision: YES**

**Risk Profile: MEDIUM (acceptably mitigatable through subcontracting pathway, multi-channel diversification, and proven distributor model)**
