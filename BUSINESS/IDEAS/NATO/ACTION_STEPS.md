# NATO Supply - Immediate Action Steps
## Cooperativa Gospodarii de Altadata (CUI 51957925)

---

## STEP 1: GET NCAGE CODE (Do First - Required for everything)

**What:** NATO Commercial and Government Entity code - 5 character ID
**Cost:** FREE
**Time:** 10 days (routine) or 3 days (emergency)
**Format for Romania:** #***L (number + alphanumeric + letter)

### How to Apply:
1. Go to: https://eportal.nspa.nato.int/Codification/CageTool/home
2. Fill out online application
3. Submit

### Contact if issues:
- Email: ncage@roncb.ro
- Website: https://bnc.defense.ro/ncage_en.html

---

## STEP 2: REGISTER AS NSPA VENDOR (Do in parallel)

**What:** Get into NSPA Source File database
**Cost:** FREE
**Required for:** All NATO procurement opportunities

### How to Register:
1. Go to: https://eportal.nspa.nato.int/VendorRegistration/
2. Complete online registration form
3. NSPA validates with Romanian authorities
4. Account created in NSPA eProcurement

### After Registration:
- Access eBid (electronic bidding system)
- See Future Business Opportunities (FBOs)
- Bid on RFPs

### Contact:
- Email: source-file@nspa.nato.int

## STEP 5: REGISTER ON SAM.GOV (For US contracts)

**What:** System for Award Management - US government contractor database
**Cost:** FREE
**Time:** 12-15 business days
**Prerequisite:** NCAGE code (Step 1)

### How to Register:
1. Get NCAGE code first
2. Go to: https://sam.gov/
3. Create account
4. Complete registration with:
   - Company information
   - NCAGE code
   - Banking info (US bank required - see workarounds below)

### US Bank Workaround Options:
1. **Mercury** - Online US bank for international businesses
2. **Wise Business** - USD account with US routing number
3. **Payoneer** - US receiving account
4. **Partner with US company** - They receive payment, transfer to you

### NAICS Codes to Register:
- 722310 - Food Service Contractors
- 311999 - All Other Miscellaneous Food Manufacturing
- 424490 - Other Grocery and Related Products Merchant Wholesalers
- 445110 - Supermarkets and Other Grocery Stores
