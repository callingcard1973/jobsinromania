# Research: MULTI-COUNTRY JOBFAIR (IDEA-117)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Replicate Norway model for Finland, Denmark, Germany, Netherlands. Same infrastructure. EUR 15K-30K/fair x 5 countries x 4 fairs/year = EUR 300K-600K/year.

## Market Size
- European recruitment fair market: multi-billion (part of EUR 30B+ EU staffing market)
- EURES European Job Days: ~50 events/year across EU (free, government-funded)
- Private recruitment fairs: EUR 5K-50K/event revenue depending on scale
- Labor shortages in target countries: FI (construction, healthcare), DK (green energy, food), DE (everything), NL (logistics, agriculture)

## Competitors
| Competitor | Scale | Model |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| EURES European Job Days | 50+ events/yr | Free, EU-funded, multi-country |
| SRT Fairs | 15+ years | University recruitment tours |
| Search Associates | Global | Education sector job fairs |
| Antal International | Multi-country | Private recruitment fairs, executive |
| National PES (each country) | Domestic | Government employment services |
| Private staffing agencies | Per-country | Own recruitment events |

## Our Advantage
- Already have infra for Norway (buildjobs.eu, pages, data)
- 28 .eu domains cover multiple sectors
- 208M company DB includes FI/DK/DE/NL companies
- Email capacity (6,300/day) for multi-country outreach
- Can run virtual fairs (IDEA-092) = zero venue cost
- OpenData already downloaded for multiple countries

## Validated?
Partially. EURES proves multi-country demand. Private sector gap for blue-collar focused cross-border fairs.

## Price
- EUR 300-500/stand per country fair
- EUR 1,000-2,000/stand for multi-country "European Tour" package
- Sponsor packages: EUR 2,000-5,000

## Risk
High. Scaling to 5 countries requires local knowledge, language support, legal compliance per country. EURES is free = hard to compete on price. Each country has different labor laws, work permits. Operational complexity x5.

## Recommendation
WAIT. Prove Norway model first (IDEA-092 + IDEA-096). Only expand after 3 successful Norway fairs with measurable ROI. Then add 1 country at a time (DE first = largest market). EUR 300K-600K projection is optimistic; realistic year 1: EUR 30K-60K from Norway alone.
