# Research: DELECROIX (IDEA-042)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Benzi transportoare recoltare Franta - distribuitor Agri Alianta, 10% comision. Tudor = business finder for Delecroix (FR, 13 employees) producing harvesting conveyor belts, vegetable trailers, sorting stations. Agri Alianta = contracted distributor (6 branches RO).

## Market Size & Demand
- European vegetable harvesting equipment: niche segment within EUR 15-20B EU agricultural machinery market
- Romania agricultural machinery imports: ~EUR 800M/year, growing with EU CAP subsidies
- Target: Romanian vegetable growers (leek, cabbage, broccoli, lettuce in Olt, Dambovita, Teleorman, Ilfov)
- Key driver: EU funds for farm mechanization (PNDR, PAC 2023-2027) -- farmers get 50-90% subsidies

## Our Advantage
- **Competitors gone**: SIMON (DE) site down, Krukowiak (PL) exited, Domasz (PL) exited
- **Price position**: Delecroix is affordable vs MTS-Sandei and Dutch manufacturers
- **Distribution locked**: Agri Alianta contract signed, 6 branches covers Romania
- **EU subsidies**: Romanian farmers can claim 50-90% reimbursement on mechanization
- **Local knowledge**: Tudor knows the Romanian agri market, contacts, language

## Market Validated?
YES -- Delecroix already exports to Belgium, Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Bulgaria, Italy, Russia, Korea. Demand for affordable harvesting belts in Romania is real (vegetable farming is labor-intensive, mechanization trend strong with EU funds).

## Price Point
- Harvesting conveyor belt: ~EUR 2,000-5,000/unit
- Vegetable trailer: ~EUR 5,000-15,000/unit
- Sorting station: ~EUR 10,000-25,000/unit
- Tudor commission: ~10% = EUR 200-2,500/unit sold
- Estimated annual: EUR 5K-60K (5-35 units x ~EUR 1,500 avg commission)

## Risk
- LOW-MEDIUM overall
- Agri Alianta may not push sales aggressively (many product lines)
- Romanian farmers are price-sensitive and slow to adopt
- Seasonal demand (equipment bought before harvest Mar-Sep)
- Single supplier dependency (only Delecroix)

## Recommendation
PROCEED. Competitive landscape uniquely favorable -- 3 of 6 competitors exited. Delecroix is the affordable European option. Next steps: (1) Get exact price list from Toubeaux, (2) Coordinate with Agri Alianta on first demos, (3) Target vegetable clusters in Olt/Dambovita/Teleorman with EU funds angle. Low effort, passive income.
