# Research: UPSELL RECOMMENDER (IDEA-051)
Date: 2026-04-16

## Description
Post-flight upsell engine: recommend hotels (Booking.com 25-40% commission) + activities (GetYourGuide 8%) to travelers after booking flights.

## Market Size
- Global affiliate marketing: ~$20B in 2026
- Travel affiliate segment: 13-16% of total = $2.6-3.2B
- Travel affiliates earn avg $13,847/mo (2nd highest niche after e-learning)
- Transportation segment: fastest CAGR to 2033

## Competitors

| Platform | Commission | Model | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|-------|
| Booking.com | 25-40% | Per booking | Tiered, higher volume = higher % |
| GetYourGuide | 8% | Per booking | Activities, tours |
| Viator | 8% | Per booking | TripAdvisor owned |
| Travelpayouts | Aggregator | Varies | Single dashboard, multiple brands |
| Klook | 2-5% | Per booking | Asia-focused activities |
| TripAdvisor | 50% rev share | CPC | Click-based, not booking |
| Skyscanner | CPA | Per click-out | Flight meta-search |

## Our Advantage
- Already have flight traffic from air ticket campaigns (694K emails)
- Post-booking email = highest intent moment
- Travelpayouts aggregates all programs in one API
- Zero inventory risk (pure affiliate)
- Can automate: flight destination -> matching hotels + activities

## Validated?
PARTIALLY -- Commission rates confirmed. Cross-sell post-booking is standard in OTAs. Gap: independent affiliates rarely do it systematically.

## Price
Booking 25-40% of hotel (~EUR 37-60 per booking). GYG 8% (~EUR 4 per activity). Blended EUR 20-40 per upsell conversion.

## Risk
- MEDIUM: Depends on flight booking volume
- Cookie duration matters (Booking.com 30 days)
- Need compelling email timing (within 1h of flight confirmation)

## Recommendation
BUILD AFTER AIR TICKETS LIVE -- Natural extension. Implement as automated post-booking email sequence via Travelpayouts API.
