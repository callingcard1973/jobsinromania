# InterJob Pipeline Funnel — 2026-04-18

## Funnel Overview

```
OUTREACH (campaigns)
    ↓
RESPONSES (response_tracker.py — 19 inboxes, every 5 min)
    ↓
CLASSIFICATION (INTERESTED / WORKER_APPLICATION / BOUNCE)
    ↓
    ├── EMPLOYER INTERESTED → Solonet draft order (solonet_pipeline.py)
    │       ↓ Tudor approves /send_solonet_X
    │       ↓ Solonet responds
    │       ↓ Workers placed → /solonet_placed_X <n> <eur>
    │       ↓ REVENUE
    │
    └── WORKER APPLICATION → master_applicants.db (worker_router.py)
            ↓ Auto-reply with interjob.ro/apply.html
            ↓ Tudor reviews
            ↓ Match to employer order
            ↓ PLACEMENT + REVENUE
```
