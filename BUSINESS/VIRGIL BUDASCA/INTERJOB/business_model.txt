# InterJob Business Model — 2026

## Mission
Make money moving labor: Asian/African workers → Romania, or Romanian teams → Scandinavia/rich EU.

---

## Revenue Stream 1: Import (Asia/Africa → Romania)

**Model:** Placement fee per worker placed with Romanian employer

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Target sectors | Construction, factory, meat, warehouse, care, horeca |
| Fee per worker | €500–€1,500 (depends on sector + contract length) |
| Volume target | 10–50 workers/month |
| Monthly revenue | €5K–€75K |
| Key bottleneck | Romanian employer with valid work authorization |

**Pipeline:**
- Employers found via ANOFM campaigns (2,249/day outreach)
- Workers from: `master_applicants.db` (758 workers), interjob.ro/apply.html
- Solonet handles work permit logistics → forward fee to solonet.vacancy@gmail.com

---

## Revenue Stream 2: Export (Romanian teams → EU/Scandinavia)

**Model:** Commission from employer OR margin on worker salary

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Target countries | Norway, Finland, Sweden, Denmark, Netherlands, Germany |
| Target sectors | Construction, welding, scaffolding, pipe-fitting, electricians |
| Commission | 10–15% of first-year gross salary |
| Per worker value | €3K–€8K |
| Volume target | 5–20 workers/month |
| Monthly revenue | €15K–€160K |

**Pipeline:**
- Employers via EBRD campaigns (150/day), TED procurement, Norway pages on buildjobs.eu/no/
- Workers from master_applicants.db, interjob.ro, ANOFM referrals
- Active campaigns: norway_virgil (10/day), FI_TED_CONSTRUCTION (80/day)

## Key Constraints

1. Romanian employers need valid work authorization before hiring non-EU workers
2. Solonet is current logistics partner — margin depends on their terms
3. Outreach limited by Brevo bounce rate (<30% threshold)
4. Worker pipeline thinner than employer pipeline — need more applicants
