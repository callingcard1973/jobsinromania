THE 7 WRITING STYLES
================================================================================

1. SETH GODIN (300-500 words)
   Voice: Provocative, conversational, makes you think
   Audience: Thinkers, decision-makers
   Platform: Medium, LinkedIn, Substack
   Hook: Single counterintuitive insight
   CTA: Implicit (makes reader think)

2. HEMINGWAY (400-600 words)
   Voice: Minimalist, sparse, powerful
   Audience: Readers who value clarity
   Platform: Twitter threads, LinkedIn, Blog
   Hook: Vivid scene, authentic moment
   CTA: Implicit (subtext speaks)

3. NY TIMES (1000-1500 words)
   Voice: Narrative journalism, human-centered
   Audience: Readers who want the full story
   Platform: Medium, Dev.to, Blog
   Hook: Real person + compelling problem
   CTA: Call for further thought/discussion

4. SCIENTIFIC (1200-1800 words)
   Voice: Formal, evidence-based, rigorous
   Audience: Academics, decision-makers, data-driven
   Platform: HashNode, Dev.to, Academia.edu
   Hook: Research question
   CTA: Methodology transparency, citations

5. PERSUASIVE/COPY (600-800 words)
   Voice: Direct, benefit-focused, urgency
   Audience: Buyers, investors, decision-makers
   Platform: LinkedIn, Email, Sales pages
   Hook: Problem statement + cost
   CTA: Clear (Book call, Apply investor, Buy dataset)

6. TWEETSTORM (10-20 tweets × 280 chars)
   Voice: Punchy, energetic, shareable
   Audience: Twitter, Threads, Mastodon users
   Platform: Twitter/X, Threads, Mastodon
   Hook: Surprising stat or question
   CTA: Retweet, follow, visit link

7. LONG-FORM SEO (2000-3000 words)
   Voice: Educational, comprehensive, keyword-rich
   Audience: Search engines, researchers
   Platform: WordPress, Ghost, Search results
   Hook: "Complete guide: ..."
   CTA: Multiple (Gumroad, newsletter, book call)

Each style targets different platforms, audiences, and objectives.
