# EBRD PSD Scraper - Project State
# Generated 2026-04-03

## What
Scraping all EBRD (European Bank for Reconstruction and Development) Project
Summary Document pages to extract company contacts, project details, and financing info.

## Source
https://www.ebrd.com/home/work-with-us/projects/psd/{ID}.html
PSD IDs range: 40000-57000 (~17,000 to check)

## What We Extract Per Project
- psd_id, project_id
- title (project name)
- country, sector, notice_type (State/Private)
- status (Exploratory/Signed/Implementing/Complete/Repaying)
- ebrd_finance (amount), finance_desc (loan/equity description)
- total_cost
- overview (full project description, up to 1000 chars)
- contact_name, contact_email, contact_phone, contact_website, contact_address
- url (source page)

## Existing Data (before scrape)
- ebrd_private_projects.csv: 7,745 private sector projects from official XLSX download
- ebrd_all_companies.csv: 1,915 unique company names
- Romania: 453 projects, EUR 9.2B total investment
- Total EBRD: 8,818 projects, EUR 214B (1991-2024)
- Top countries: Russia 22.1B, Turkiye 20.8B, Ukraine 16B, Poland 14.1B, Romania 9.2B

## Scraper Setup
- Script: /opt/ACTIVE/SCRAPERS/EBRD/ebrd_psd_scraper.py (on raspibig)
- Skill: /opt/ACTIVE/INFRA/SKILLS/ebrd_psd_scraper.py
- Output CSV: /opt/ACTIVE/SCRAPERS/EBRD/data/ebrd_psd_details.csv
- State: /opt/ACTIVE/SCRAPERS/EBRD/data/psd_state.json (resumes on restart)
- Node-RED: "EBRD Scraper" tab, checks every 5 min, auto-restarts
- Delay: 10-30 sec random between requests, rotating user agents
- Saves: every 5 records to CSV + state file
- Estimated: ~4 days to complete all 17K IDs

## Data Quality
- Not all PSD pages have company contacts (some are state/sovereign projects)
- Contact info in "Company Contact Information" section: name, email, phone, website, address
- All output in ASCII
- IDs are not sequential (many 404 gaps)
