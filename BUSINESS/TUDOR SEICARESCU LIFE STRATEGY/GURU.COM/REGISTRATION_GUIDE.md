# Freelance Platform Registration Guide

Open each link, register, then paste the pre-written text below.

---

## 1. GURU.COM

**Register**: https://www.guru.com/registeraccount.aspx
- Click Sign Up > Google login > Select "Freelancer" > Proceed
- Free tier: 10 bids/month, 9% fee
- Basic+ ($8.95/yr): more bids, 7% fee

**After registration, set up profile:**
- Profile > Edit Profile > Add tagline, summary, skills, hourly rate

---

## 2. UPWORK

**Register**: https://www.upwork.com/nx/signup/?dest=home
- Sign up with Google > Choose "Work as a freelancer"
- Free tier: 10 Connects/month (each proposal costs 2-6 Connects)

---

## 3. FIVERR

**Register**: https://www.fiverr.com/join
- Sign up with Google > Complete profile
- Create "Gigs" (productized services with fixed prices)

---

## 4. FREELANCER.COM

**Register**: https://www.freelancer.com/signup
- Sign up with email/Google > Select freelancer
- Free: 8 bids/month

---

## 5. PEOPLEPERHOUR

**Register**: https://www.peopleperhour.com/freelancer
- Sign up > Create "Hourlies" (fixed-price offers)
- EU-focused, good for European clients

---

## 6. ARC.DEV (bonus - curated, higher rates)

**Apply**: https://arc.dev/for-developers
- Application-based, 72h matching, senior-level only
- Higher rates ($50-100+/hr)
