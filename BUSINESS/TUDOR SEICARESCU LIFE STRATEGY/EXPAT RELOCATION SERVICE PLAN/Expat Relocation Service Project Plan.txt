

    
    Project: Expat Relocation Service (Romania)

    
    1. Overview

    Build a digital-first relocation and concierge service targeting primarily US expats moving to Romania. The business will combine productized service packages, subscription-based support, and a marketplace of verified providers.

    

    
    
    2. Target Market

    Primary: US remote workers, digital nomads, entrepreneurs

    Secondary: EU expats relocating for cost-of-living advantages

    Customer characteristics:

    High willingness to pay

    Low tolerance for bureaucracy

    Preference for done-for-you services

    

    
    
    3. Core Value Proposition

    “We make Romania work for you.”

    Key promise:

    Eliminate bureaucracy friction

    Provide fast, clear, English-first processes

    Offer end-to-end relocation support

    

    
    
    4. Service Offerings

    
    4.1 Packages

    Landing Pack (€300–€800)

    Airport pickup

    SIM card setup

    Bank account assistance

    Basic orientation

    Legal Stay Pack (€500–€1500)

    Residency permit assistance

    Tax ID (NIF) setup

    Health insurance registration

    Full Relocation (€1500–€5000)

    Housing support

    Utilities setup

    Full documentation handling

    End-to-end onboarding

    

    
    
    4.2 Micro-Services

    ANAF appointments

    Company (SRL) setup

    Rental contract negotiation

    Driver’s license exchange

    Healthcare registration

    

    
    
    4.3 Subscription Model

    Expat Support Membership (€20–€100/month)

    Ongoing support (chat/WhatsApp)

    Admin reminders (renewals, deadlines)

    Priority assistance

    Partner discounts (legal, accounting)

    

    
    
    
    5. Platform Layer (expatsinromania.org)

    Transform site into:

    Service marketplace (lawyers, accountants, agents)

    Verified providers

    Review system

    Lead generation + commissions

    

    
    
    6. Go-To-Market Strategy

    
    6.1 Content Funnel

    “Moving from the US to Romania”

    “Cost of living comparison”

    “Romania digital nomad visa guide”

    Goal: Capture organic search traffic

    

    
    
    6.2 Conversion

    Clear service packages

    Online booking + payment (Stripe)

    Fast onboarding (WhatsApp/email)

    

    
    
    6.3 Positioning

    Primary angle: “We help Americans relocate to Romania without dealing with Romanian bureaucracy.”

    

    
    
    
    7. MVP Plan

    Phase 1: 1. Create 3 core packages 2. Add payment system 3. Add contact channel (WhatsApp) 4. Publish 10 SEO articles

    Phase 2: 1. Build provider marketplace 2. Add subscription system 3. Automate document workflows

    

    
    
    8. Revenue Streams

    Service packages (one-time)

    Subscription (recurring)

    Commission from providers

    

    
    
    9. Competitive Advantage

    Digital-first approach

    Clear pricing

    Fast execution

    Niche focus (US expats)

    

    
    
    10. Risks

    Bureaucratic unpredictability

    Dependency on local partners

    Competition scaling quickly

    Mitigation:

    Build strong partner network

    Standardize processes

    Focus on user experience

    

    
    
    11. Long-Term Vision

    Become the main entry point for expats moving to Romania

    Expand to other Eastern European countries

    Build automation layer for relocation processes

    

    
    
    12. Next Actions

    Define pricing precisely

    Set up landing page

    Validate demand with first clients

    Build partnerships with lawyers/accountants
