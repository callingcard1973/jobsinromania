                                                            WELCOME IN HORTECH

                                                            Since 2004, our company manufactures machineries for soil preparation, transplanting, harvest and plants protection. Technological innovation is one of our key points with research and development, to create innovative machineries more and more sustainables. We are leaders in the production of horticultural machineries, products which guarantee for high performance and quality in all over the world.
