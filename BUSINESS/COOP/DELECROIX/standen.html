Standen is the leading UK manufacturer and worldwide exporter of potato planting and harvesting machinery which is all manufactured in the city of Ely in Cambridgeshire, where we have been making British machinery since 1846. Standen Imports is our imported machinery division, specialising in vegetable and salad planting and harvesting machines. 

Standen T2xs Potato Harvester

The Standen T2xs Harvester sees the best-selling Standen T2 2-row trailed harvester re-engineered for 2020. 

The overhaul has delivered a 15% increase in the separation area, new haulm roller design, new touch screen controls, a wider choice of separators, improved service access, wider choice of picking table options and 45% more machine levelling.
